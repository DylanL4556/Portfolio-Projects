Prepare for Your Future in Professional Fields
Welcome to a vibrant community at USCA, where students collaborate with a dedicated team of faculty members to pursue a degree from a range of fields. Our diverse degree options are tailored to spark curiosity and foster expertise. At USCA you can choose a degree program that fits your interests in biology, earth science, clinical laboratory science, or public health. Whether you're pursuing a Bachelor of Science or Bachelor of Arts, or specializing with a concentration in Environmental Remediation and Restoration or Molecular Biology, we have a path that will align with your ambitions.
Choose from an array of exciting biology degrees that include concentrations in Molecular Biology and Environmental Remediation & Restoration. Biology majors may also choose to incorporate courses from our pre-Medicine, pre-Dentistry, or pre-Veterinary Medicine tracks into their degree program. For those drawn to studying earth, marine, or atmospheric science, our Bachelor of Science in Environmental Earth Systems offers a deep dive into environmental and earth systems studies. Those interested in careers related to health care may choose from our Public Health or Clinical Laboratory Science programs.
You can learn more about our degree options on our Programs page or in our Student Handbook located in Resources.
Research that Resonates
Research isn't just part of the curriculum—it's at the heart of your educational journey at USCA. Engage in meaningful, independent laboratory projects alongside your professors. These projects not only complement your coursework but are also designed to deepen your practical expertise. Our students frequently co-author peer-reviewed articles and present their groundbreaking findings at conferences at various levels.
Real-World Experience
At USCA, we believe that the best way to learn is by doing. Our coursework is enriched with extensive lab work, placing theory into practice. This hands-on approach not only solidifies your understanding but also sharpens your skills, preparing you to excel in the competitive scientific arena, whether in advanced studies or your future career.
