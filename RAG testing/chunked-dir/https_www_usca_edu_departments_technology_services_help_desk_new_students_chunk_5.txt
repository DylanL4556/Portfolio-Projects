Welcome to the Technology Services Help Desk, your one-stop destination for technical support!
Our dedicated team is committed to providing unparalleled assistance to students, faculty, and staff, ensuring a seamless and enriching experience with our digital resources. Whether you’re encountering a software snag, needing hardware guidance, or seeking general IT advice, we’re here to lend a helping hand. Appointments are available all year, including throughout the summer! Instead of waiting until classes start, stop by any time for assistance! To ensure someone will be here to help you quickly, be sure to call or email for an appointment.
At the Help Desk, our mission is simple: to empower our university community by delivering reliable, efficient, and personalized support. Let us be your trusted partners on your technological journey because excellence begins with assistance at USCA.
