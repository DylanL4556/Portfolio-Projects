I. Policy
- All unclassified employees of USC Aiken must have annual performance appraisals. Full-time faculty members are appraised according to procedures in the USCA Faculty Manual Section 5.7. Unclassified employees with senior administrative responsibilities are appraised on the Administrator Evaluation form. Unclassified employees who are neither full-time faculty, nor senior administrators may be appraised using the EPMS form for classified employees or other method agreed upon by the employee and supervisor.
