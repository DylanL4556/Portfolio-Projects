Services & Programs for Veteran & Military Students
The Office of Veteran & Military Student Success (VMSS) provides services and support to veteran students, military family member students, and those students currently in service of our country’s Armed Forces in their educational pursuits as they transition from their military service through the university and to becoming vital community members.
