If there is still space on the waitlist for a course, you can add yourself to the waitlist for a course in Self Service Aiken by adding the section to your course summary, selecting “Waitlist” from the dropdown menu, and then clicking Submit.
Waitlists will remain active until the course add/drop deadline of each semester (about one week after the semester begins). If you are on the waitlist for a course and a spot becomes available for you, then you will receive an automated email to your USCA email informing you that you have 24 hours to register for the course in Self Service Aiken.
