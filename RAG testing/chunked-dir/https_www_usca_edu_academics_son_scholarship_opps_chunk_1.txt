USCA School of Nursing Scholarship Opportunities
Each academic year the School of Nursing awards thousands of dollars in scholarships to students enrolled in both BSN program tracks (traditional BSN program and RN-BSN program). To be considered for one of many scholarships, students should complete the Honors, Awards, and Scholarships (H.A.S.) Application no later than February 1 at 1700.
The School of Nursing Student Support Committee reviews all scholarship applications in March or April. Students will be notified if they will receive a scholarship in April or May.
Complete the application form below. Please contact Ms. Kathy Simmons, School of Nursing Academic Advisor, with any questions.
