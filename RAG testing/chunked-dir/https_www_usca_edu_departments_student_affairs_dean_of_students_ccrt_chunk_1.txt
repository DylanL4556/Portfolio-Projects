The Community Concerns Response Team (CCRT) encourages responsible behaviors to mitigate risk by responding proactively to reported or noted concerns and coordinating appropriate support from campus and community resources for individuals, student groups, and student organizations. This team, made up of students, faculty, and staff, specifically works with members of our campus community to help them understand USCA policies and procedures in any situation.
