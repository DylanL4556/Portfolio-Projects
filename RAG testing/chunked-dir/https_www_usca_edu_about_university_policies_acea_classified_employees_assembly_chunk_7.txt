- The criteria for selecting the four Classified Employees of the Year are as follows:

- This award will be based on Jan. 1 - Dec. 31 of the previous year only
- Community Service criteria:
- Must be employed by USCA for 2 years by January 1 of the award year
- Name of organization, position held, and dates
- Contributions (not monetary) to the organization
- University Service criteria:
- Must be employed by USCA for 2 years by January 1 of the award year
- This award is to be based on services rendered during any period while employed by USCA
- Dedication to USCA (positive approach to USCA life)
- Service outside of immediate job duties (examples): committee memberships, volunteers for special USCA functions and events, and student activities
- Significantly helps to enhance the image of USCA

II. Procedure
- Nominations may come from any source other than self-nominations. Nominations are limited to one per award, therefore it is allowable for an individual to nominate up to four employees for Classified Employee of the Year, one for Community Service, and one for University Service.
- The classified Employees Awards Committee will consist of the CEA Advisory Committee Members and one member appointed by the Executive Committee who will serve as a non-voting member to provide guidance to the committee. This member will be knowledgeable of the award process. The appointed person will be a CEA of the Year award recipient from the previous two years, therefore, being ineligible to receive the award.
