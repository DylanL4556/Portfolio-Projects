The Ruth Patrick Science Education Center is pleased to announce we will be hosting Homeschool Mondays for Spring 2026.
The RPSEC series of monthly programs for homeschool students are held on select Monday afternoons from January to May. Each student will participate in 2 one-hour-long programs focusing on science, technology, or mathematics.
Registration
Please use the link below to register your student(s). This form will be electronically sent to the RPSEC upon completion.
Depending on your choice of payment, you will either bring cash, mail or bring a check, or the RPSEC will contact you for credit card payments.
The cost is $50.00 per student BEFORE the early registration deadline of November 14, 2025, by 5:00 p.m.
The cost is $65.00 per student AFTER the early registration deadline of November 14, 2025 after 5:00 pm.

RPSEC also welcomes parents to stay, and we appreciate having at least one parent volunteer per 8 children for each group of students.
There is no charge for adults to participate in our Homeschool Mondays programs. Please note that children under the age of 4 are not permitted.
