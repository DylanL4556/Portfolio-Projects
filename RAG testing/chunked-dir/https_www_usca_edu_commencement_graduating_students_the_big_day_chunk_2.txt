Class Gift
Along with graduation, graduates may become a member of the Reins of Legacy. For a $25 donation (pledge to commit $25 or more for 2 additional years), graduates will receive philanthropic cords to wear at graduation, a special alumni gift, a certificate, and a one-year membership to the Alumni Association and gain access to the Pacer Perks program.
There will also be a special induction ceremony for family and guests to attend.
To learn more about Reins of Legacy, please visit https://www.usca.edu/departments/alumni/senior-class-gift/
