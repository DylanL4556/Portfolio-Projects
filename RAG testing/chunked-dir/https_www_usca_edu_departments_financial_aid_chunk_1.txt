Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
The 2025-2026 FAFSA is needed for Fall 2025, Spring 2026 and Summer 2026. It is available to be submitted to FSA through June 30, 2026.
The 2026-27 FAFSA is now available for students and families to submit for the 2026-27 academic year (Fall 2026, Spring 2027, and Summer 2027).
We encourage USCA students to complete their FAFSA during winter break and come by our office for any help.
USCA’s 2025-26 FAFSA Priority Filing Date is March 1, 2025. This is a priority date, not a deadline. If you cannot meet the priority date, we encourage you to complete the FAFSA as early as possible, even after March 1.
Welcome to the USCA Office of Financial Aid. We are committed to helping you navigate the financial aid process and access the necessary resources to fund your education.
Whether you’re a prospective student, a current student, or a family member, our goal is to provide clear, comprehensive information about your financial aid options.
Understanding Your Options
At USCA, we offer a wide range of financial aid options to support your educational journey, including:
- Scholarships: Discover merit-based and need-based scholarships available for incoming and current students. Explore our scholarship portal for opportunities tailored to your academic achievements, community service, and unique talents.
- Grants: Learn about federal, state, and institutional grants like the Pell Grant and the South Carolina Need-Based Grant, which provide financial assistance that does not need to be repaid.
- Loans: Familiarize yourself with federal and private loan options, including Direct Subsidized and Unsubsidized Loans, PLUS Loans, and alternative loan choices. Understand the terms, interest rates, and repayment plans to make informed decisions.
- Work-Study Programs: Gain valuable work experience while earning money to help pay for your education through our Federal Work-Study Program. Explore available on-campus positions and community service opportunities.
