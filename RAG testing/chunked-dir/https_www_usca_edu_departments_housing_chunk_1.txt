Your Home Away from Home!
We are ready for University Housing to become your home while you pursue your academic journey at USCA.
Check out our three living communities:
Pacer Crossings
Pacer Crossings houses our incoming freshmen. Each suite contains four furnished bedrooms, four full bathrooms, and a furnished living room. Every bedroom also comes furnished with a mini fridge and a microwave. The laundry room is on the second floor, and the Learning Center, which has computers, individual study rooms, and a printer, is on the third floor.
Pacer Commons
Pacer Commons offers apartment-style housing with double rooms and single rooms. However, single rooms are primarily for upperclassmen. Each apartment contains two bedrooms, two full bathrooms, a furnished living room, and a fully equipped kitchen. The laundry room is on the first floor, and the computer lab is on the third floor. There is a volleyball court behind Pacer Commons for easy access.
Pacer Downs
Pacer Downs offers apartment housing with double rooms and double-as-single rooms for upperclassmen and graduate students. Double-as-a-single rooms are available on a limited basis and are reserved for upperclassmen and graduate students. Each apartment contains two bedrooms, two full bathrooms, a furnished living area, and a fully equipped kitchen. There is an on-site laundry room, a volleyball court, a basketball hoop, and a furnished Community Center complete with a computer lab and printer.
Apply for Housing
Applying for University Housing is simple. Just have your USCA email address and updated password ready to log into the Housing Portal to complete and submit your Housing Application.
