The Strategic Leadership and Innovation Circle is an advocate, advisor, and thought partner to USCA's Dean of the College of Arts, Humanities, and Social Sciences. The Circle champions an education that blends liberal arts with career preparation, provides guidance for program development, and fosters community, industry, and employer relationships that enable students to explore their passions and pursue meaningful careers. Through advocacy, fundraising, and dedicated service, members actively promote the continued growth and success of the College.
