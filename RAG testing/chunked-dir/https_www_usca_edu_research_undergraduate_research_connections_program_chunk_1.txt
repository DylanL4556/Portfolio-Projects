Connections Award
The USCA Center for Research Excellence sponsors the Connections Award to promote the formation of research and creative interactions between our students and faculty. The Connections program will provide funding, up to $1000, for mentored undergraduate scholarly and creative activities.
All USCA undergraduates with a GPA of 3.0 or higher interested in pursuing research or creative activities with a USCA faculty member are encouraged to apply, regardless of major. The Call for Proposals provides specific details about this program.
We invite all interested students and their mentors to participate.

Connections Student Travel Award
In addition to the Connections Student Travel application, three forms are also required and must be submitted on the online application.
- A brief personal statement, written by the student, describes the intended presentation's nature and how participation will further the student’s academic career.
- An invitation/acceptance letter for the presentation is required. If notification of acceptance has not been received before the application deadline, please attach proof of submission.
- A completed signature form
