The Etherredge Center is proud to be able to host the events that have been presented over the years, however, we need your support to continue to bring superior arts programming to the CSRA. Your assistance will also enable the educational mission of the Etherredge Center to flourish as we provide workshops and outreach programs to our students and community.
It is the mission of the Etherredge Center to be an enduring place for diverse, quality artistic performance and exhibitions, a place for community arts collaboration and growth, and a place where the arts stimulate the thinking and emotions of the residents of the CSRA.
