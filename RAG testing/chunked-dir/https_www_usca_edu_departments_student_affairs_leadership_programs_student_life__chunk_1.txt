About the Leadership Awards Banquet
Annually sponsored by the Office of Student Life, the Student Leadership Awards Banquet recognizes outstanding student body, faculty, and staff leaders. Numerous awards represent our students, faculty, and staff's time and dedication towards our university to make it a great place to be! See the complete list of awards below. For many of the awards, the individual must be nominated. If you have any questions, contact Logan Sharpe at Logan.Sharpe@usca.edu.
