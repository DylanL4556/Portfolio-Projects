You can pay for the room with cash, check, credit card, or debit card when you check into the room.
Items to Bring
Please bring all your needed toiletries--shampoo/conditioner, soap, toothpaste--and anything else to make your stay on campus more comfortable. The lounges in Pacer Crossings have televisions you can use during your stay on campus.
How to Make a Reservation
Please complete the Housing Orientation Reservation Form to submit your request. Please submit the housing reservation form at least one week (seven days) before the orientation session that you will be attending to provide University Housing the time needed to set up your room.
Please contact University Housing at housing@usca.edu or call (803) 641-3790 if you have any questions.
