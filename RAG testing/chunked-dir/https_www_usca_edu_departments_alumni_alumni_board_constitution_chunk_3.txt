. Election of Officers
- The President, Vice President, and Secretary/Treasurer shall be elected every other year, at the final annual meeting of the USCA Alumni Association by a majority of those active members attending that meeting or voting in writing by email or other written means, received by the Secretary prior to the meeting. There shall be only one vote per Active Member allowed.
- Prior to the Final Meeting, a Nominating Committee appointed by the Executive Committee. composed of at least three members of the Alumni Board shall compile and present in writing a slate of officers to the active members of the Board at least two weeks prior to the final meeting.
- All nominees must be Active Members of the USCA Alumni Association.
- All nominees so presented must give their prior consent to have their names placed in nomination.
- Any active member of the Association attending the final meeting is eligible for nomination from the floor to an office.
D. Vacancies
- In addition the provisions in IV.A. 1., a vacancy in the existing terms of any Association office, except President, shall be filled by appointment of the President until the next Annual or Special Association Meeting
- At the Final Meeting a permanent successor shall be elected by majority vote as provided in IV.C. Herein above.
ARTICLE V: ALUMNI BOARD
A. The Alumni Board shall be an advisory council for and represent the Alumni Association in all matters; carry out the mission of the Association; receive and act on reports; set annual fees for Active Membership in the Association; establish levels of membership in addition to those provided herein above; and carry out such other duties and responsibilities as are necessary for the functioning of the Association. Ideally the Alumni Board will be comprised of up to fifteen (15) members who must be active members of the Association and shall serve terms of two (2) years.
B. Members
- Members shall be appointed to the Alumni Board by the Executive Committee,
- The Alumni Board should strive to have representatives from each of the colleges and professional schools.
- Other at-large members may be appointed by the President with the advice and consent of the Alumni Board. The Alumni Board shall strive to achieve a diversity of membership in the Alumni Council that reflects the diversity of current and former students of USCA.
- The Presidents’ (or a designee) from any approved Alumni Council (see Amendment I) shall serve as at-large members to the Alumni Board.
