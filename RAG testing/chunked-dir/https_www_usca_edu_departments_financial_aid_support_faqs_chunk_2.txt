To be considered for the State Need-Based Grant at USCA, you must complete the FAFSA. Since funding is limited and awarded on a first come first serve basis, you will want to apply early and no later than March 1.
Some financial aid is limited, so filing early means there is a better chance to receive some limited funding. Students who wish to have financial aid in place in time to pay bills in August should have the FAFSA completed and requested documentation submitted to our office no later than July 1. For incoming new freshman students, Financial Aid Award Letters will mailed and posted on Self Service Aiken (SSA) beginning on or around March 1*.
*Must have all outstanding requirements complete.
