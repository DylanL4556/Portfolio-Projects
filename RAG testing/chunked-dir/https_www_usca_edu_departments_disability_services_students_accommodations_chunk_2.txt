Special Emergency Accommodations for Housing
Any student who qualifies for special accommodations due to a physical disability, vision, deaf, or mobile impairment should contact the Office of Disability Services at 803-643-6815. After the student goes through the Disability Service interview processes, University Housing is notified of the emergency accommodation(s), whether a single room, a refrigerator for medication, bed shakers, or a doorbell light. Fire alarms and strobe lights are already installed in the dormitories.
(Also refer to Housing Community Guide, email Housing@usca.edu or call 803-641-3790 for more information)
