How to Apply for Financial Aid
- Complete the FAFSA: The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) is your first step. USCA's school code is 003449. Ensure you meet our priority deadline to maximize your aid eligibility.
- Review Your Aid Offer: Once your FAFSA is processed, you'll receive a financial aid offer outlining your eligible scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study options.
- Accept Your Aid: Log in to your student portal to accept or decline your aid offers. Remember, you should only borrow what you need.
