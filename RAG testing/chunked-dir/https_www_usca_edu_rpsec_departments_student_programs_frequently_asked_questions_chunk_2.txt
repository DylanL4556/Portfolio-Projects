 to cancel a visit, we must receive written notification at least FOUR weeks in advance so we can fill it from our wait list. Written notification of cancellation MUST be received at least 4 weeks prior to the reserved date, or a $25.00 cancellation fee PER PROGRAM will be assessed. Groups from that school will not be permitted to visit the RPSEC until the fee has been paid.
*NOTE: An additional $10.00 PER PROGRAM will be assessed if your group misses your scheduled visit without contacting the RPSEC prior to the start time.
A “double” group consists of two groups of 30 students who rotate through a series of two programs. (MAX= 60)
Double Group Example (31-60 students, approximately 21⁄2 hours)
Divide students into two equal groups: A and B (no more than 30 students per group)
| Time | Group A | Group B |
|---|---|---|
| 9:15 –10:15 AM | Planetarium | Classroom |
| 10:30 –11:30 AM | Classroom | Planetarium |
A “triple” group consists of three groups of 30 students who rotate through a series of three programs. (MAX= 90)
Triple Group Example (61-90 students, approximately 4 hours)
Divide students into three equal groups: A, B, C (no more than 30 students per group)
| Time |
Group A | Group B | Group C |
|---|---|---|---|
| 9:15 –10:15 AM | Planetarium | Classroom 1 | Classroom 2 |
| 10:30 –11:30 AM | Classroom 1 | Classroom 2 | Planetarium |
| 11:30 –12:00 | LUNCH | LUNCH | LUNCH |
| 12:00 –1:00 | Classroom 2 | Planetarium | Classroom 1 |
We can accommodate a larger group (up to 120 students) on the same day within a shorter time frame using an “Expanded Double,” or “Expanded Triple” schedule if special arrangements are made at least 30 days in advance.
