Steps to Enroll:
- If you wish to claim in-state residency for tuition purposes, please do so by logging in to your SSA ("Self Service Aiken" system) and completing the “South Carolina Residency Certification” process (button at the top right of the SSA Student tab). Self Service Aiken is a convenient, one-stop website enabling you to conduct most student transactions with a secure connection to your academic, financial, personal, and technology pages.
- Transient students will be registered after USCA's continuing students have had the opportunity to participate in priority registration but before open registration begins for new students. You must complete the steps above before registration can take place.
- Transfer Student Initiatives and Services Office will contact you regarding the course(s) you requested.
- Pay fees. Fees may be paid online via the SSA system or in person in the Business Services Office, room 114, in the Penland Administration building.
Other Important Information
Campus Technology. For information on your USCA email account and other campus technology, visit the Help Desk website.
Books. Required texts and materials are available at the USCA Bookstore.
Parking Decals. All students must pay a public safety fee and are issued a parking decal. After you pay your bill, parking decals are available at the University Police Office, Pacer Downs Community Building, during regular office hours
ID cards. All students will be issued a USCA ID (also known as a Carolina Card). ID cards are made in the University Police Office, Pacer Downs Community Building, during regular office hours

Transferring credit back to home institution
After your course is over, you must have a USC transcript sent to your home institution to have your course credit transferred back. To request a PDF transcript, you must log in to your SSA account, select the “Student Records” tab, and click the "Request Your Electronic PDF Transcript" link.
