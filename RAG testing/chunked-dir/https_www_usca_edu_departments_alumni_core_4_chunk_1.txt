Each year, distinguished alumni who embrace the university's values —character, citizenship, curiosity, and collegiality— are selected to be part of USCA’s Core 4.
Who is Eligible?
Individuals who have obtained an associate’s, bachelor's, or master’s degree from USCA.
Alumni who have completed a substantial amount of coursework but did not graduate (i.e. engineering majors before that degree being offered at our campus, Banksia-era transfer students, etc..) are also eligible for nomination.
Graduates and alumni must embrace the university values of character, citizenship, curiosity, and collegiality.
Self-nominations will not be accepted. Current Alumni Board members are ineligible for this award.
The Process
- The Alumni Executive Board will collect nominations from October 14, 2024 through January 6, 2025 via the Core 4 Nomination Form.
- Nominations are reviewed, and eligible nominees are contacted to accept the nomination.
- Winners will be announced at USCA’s Homecoming Alumni Reception. Plaques will be awarded during an on-court ceremony during the game.
To nominate alumni who exemplify these values professionally and personally, please fill out the nomination form when it becomes available.
