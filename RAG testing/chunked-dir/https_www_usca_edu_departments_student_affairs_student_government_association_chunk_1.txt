Mission Statement:
The Student Government Association (SGA) is here to address and resolve your issues to the best of our ability.
We are confident that this year’s Senate will do an excellent job representing you. So, if you have any questions or concerns regarding topics within your major, contact your Senate representative. SGA is a “senator-serving-you” government, so feel free to stop by our office in the Student Activities Center, where your Senate officers will be more than happy to meet with you and listen to your concerns.
Call or stop by the SGA office and follow us on social media for more information.
