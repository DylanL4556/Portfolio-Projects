Scholarships at USCA
Here at USCA, we believe in making quality education accessible and affordable. That's why we're committed to providing a variety of scholarship opportunities to help fund your journey toward academic excellence and career success.
Whether you're an incoming freshman, a continuing student, or transferring from another institution, we have scholarship options that could make your education more affordable.

How to Apply
Applying for scholarships at USCA is a straightforward process. Here’s how you can get started:
- Complete the FAFSA: To be considered for financial aid, including scholarships, submit the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). USCA’s school code is 003449.
- Check Scholarship Deadlines: Each scholarship has its own application deadline. Ensure you’re aware of these dates to maximize your opportunities.
- Submit Required Documents: Some scholarships may require additional documents, such as essays or letters of recommendation. Submit all required materials by the specified deadlines.
Additional Resources
For more detailed information on specific scholarships, application processes, and deadlines, please contact the Financial Aid Office. Our team is here to assist you in navigating your scholarship options and making your education at USCA as affordable as possible.
