Should your GPA fall below the minimum requirement for the semester you just completed, you will be placed in probationary status. You may continue to take Honors courses during your probation, but you must bring your GPA back up to that required by the end of the following semester to be reinstated in the Honors Program. You will be dismissed from the program if you cannot do so.
Most Honors Program courses will be offered only during the fall and spring semesters. Some colloquia may be available during summer sessions. If you would like to take a summer Honors enriched (501) course as part of your General Education or Major requirements, please check with Dr. Michelle Vieyra.
USCA is a proud member of the National Collegiate Honors Council.
