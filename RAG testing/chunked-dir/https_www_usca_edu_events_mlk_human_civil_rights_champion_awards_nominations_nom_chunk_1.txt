The Human & Civil Rights Community Champion Award
One (1) award is to be presented to a person or organization in Aiken City or County in the area of human and civil rights.
To be eligible for the Human & Civil Rights Community Champion Award, one or more of the following criteria must be met:
- Helped protect and advance the human and civil rights of the Aiken County community.
- Be an active member of a service organization in the Aiken County community.
- Worked to build more effective partnerships with students and schools, especially Aiken Technical College and USC Aiken.
- Has publicly promoted efforts to unify the Aiken County community by promoting collaboration and protecting the rights of its citizens.
Nominations must be received by 5 p.m. December 1, 2024.
