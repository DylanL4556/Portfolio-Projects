Annual scholarships reflect a donor’s commitment to support a scholarship in a specific area or for a specific purpose. To establish an annual account, a minimum of $1,000 for three years or a total commitment of $3,000 is required.
Giving to USCA affects not only our students, but also our community and the larger world. As a public comprehensive institution, our mission is to prepare students for success by challenging them to think critically and creatively, communicate effectively, learn independently, and acquire breadth and depth of interdisciplinary knowledge. Gifts contribute to student success and help the University prepare them for careers in a diverse global environment. Today’s successful students are equipped to take their place as tomorrow’s leaders.
Scholarships, program accounts, and endowed chairs are more than dollars and cents. They allow donors to share their personal beliefs, characteristics, and aspirations with students, faculty, and staff and give back to the university that gave so much to them.
How to Donate
For more information on donating, contact the USCA Advancement Office at advancementservices@usca.edu or (803) 641-3334.
If you are a student and would like more information on how to apply for a scholarship, contact the Financial Aid Office at stuaid@usca.edu or (803) 641-3476.
You can make your gift to USCA online through the online giving site! We accept all major credit cards, including American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa. You may also designate your gift to specific areas at USCA, and you can give in memory or honor of someone.
