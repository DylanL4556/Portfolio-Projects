- Exceptional Faculty: Learn from award-winning experts, including a Carolina Trustees Professor and a SC Governor’s Professor of the Year.
- Proven Success: Enjoy a 90% placement rate into employment or advanced studies within six months of graduation, with alumni thriving in prestigious positions and top graduate programs.
