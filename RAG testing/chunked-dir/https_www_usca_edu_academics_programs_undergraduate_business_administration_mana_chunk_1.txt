Program Highlights
USCA is among the 5% of business schools worldwide to be accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB). That means, at USCA, you can expect a robust curriculum, dedicated faculty, expanded learning opportunities outside the classroom, and more.
In particular, the business degree in management—which is the most affordable in South Carolina—stands out due to its:
- Dedication to career readiness. You will graduate from our business degree in management with the knowledge and skills you need to thrive in your career. The USCA School of Business Administration (SOBA) has a presence in the Center for Career Readiness to ensure business management majors are ready to enter the workforce upon graduation.
- Leadership in research. USCA is home to the Pacer Center for Excellence in Business Research and Entrepreneurship, which provides you with an opportunity to gain research experience while pursuing your business management major. The Center was established to house the wide variety of cutting-edge research USCA does to solve real-world business issues.
- Preparation for professional certifications. You’ll gain the necessary background for a number of professional certifications, such as SAS Tier 1 academic certifications. These certifications, when paired with your bachelor’s in business management, demonstrate to employers that you have the skills they want and need, making you more marketable in the workplace.
- Opportunities for learning outside the classroom. USCA gives business management majors abundant opportunities to make their degrees their own. Take part in faculty-led study abroad programs, complete internships, do independent research, and more. Plus, attend networking events or campus lectures to hear first-hand from leaders in your field.
