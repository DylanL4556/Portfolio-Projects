Pacer Account
The account students, faculty, staff and affiliates use to access local USCA campus resources, like: the USCA campus network, @usca.edu email, campus computers, Wi-Fi, VPN, shared drives and folders, printing, and all usca.edu websites, are all now known as the Pacer Account.

For instructions on how to connect to the internet on campus, please visit the Pacer Account (Internet) topic.

To complete setup of your Pacer Account, please see the instructions on the New Students page, under Setup Accounts and Passwords.
