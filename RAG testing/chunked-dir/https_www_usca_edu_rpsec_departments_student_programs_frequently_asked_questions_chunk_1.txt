- Maximum of 30 students per hands-on discovery program (minimum = 10).
- Maximum of 60 students for a double group (students rotate through 2 programs).
- Maximum of 90 students for a triple group (students rotate through 3 programs).
- Please do not bring more than 90 students on the same day unless special arrangements have been made in advance and confirmed in writing.
Please take a headcount BEFORE you enter the RPSEC. Upon arrival, the group leader should report to the main office with the total number of students and adults. Groups should enter through the double-door entrance. Line up your students in one, two, or three even groups in the gallery (maximum of 30 students per group). Please make checks payable to USCA or inform us that you need to be invoiced.
Lunch facilities are available at the RPSEC, but you must make a reservation before your visit. Facilities are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Participants should bring bag lunches and drinks. The RPSEC does not have food available.
IMPORTANT:
- DOUBLE GROUPS - Please pre-divide your lunches and drinks to eat in two different rooms
- TRIPLE GROUPS - Please pre-divide your lunches and drinks to eat in three different rooms
Chaperones are welcome and encouraged to attend.
- There is no charge for adults attending discovery programs (non-planetarium programs).
- ONE adult per 8 students will be given free admission to planetarium programs. Adults exceeding this maximum will be charged a special adult admission price of $ 3.75 each. (RPSEC considers all teachers and teacher’s aides/assistants or student teachers along with chaperones and bus drivers as “adults”.)
- To limit distractions to learning, children under the age of 4 are not permitted to attend any student programs at the RPSEC.
- All participants should turn off cell phones and audible personal electronic devices during their visit.
If you need to cancel a visit, we must receive written notification at least FOUR weeks in advance so we can fill it from our wait list. Written notification of cancellation MUST be received at least 4 weeks prior to the reserved date, or a $25.00 cancellation fee PER PROGRAM will be assessed. Groups from that school will not be permitted to visit the RPSEC until the fee has been paid.
