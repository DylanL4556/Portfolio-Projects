USCA's undergraduate business administration program, available in-person and online, caters to new entrepreneurs and career-focused professionals.
The program stands out for three key reasons: its prestigious AACSB accreditation (held by only 5% of business schools worldwide), abundant opportunities for students to engage in impactful research with regional organizations, and highly competitive tuition rates.
These features combine to offer students a high-quality, hands-on education that advances their career prospects without breaking the bank, making USCA an attractive choice for business education in Georgia and South Carolina.
