You’ve got an interest in computers, cybersecurity, or gaming. All you need are the tools to turn that into a successful career through an NSA-designated Applied Computer Science degree.
At the University of South Carolina Aiken, our NSA-designated applied computer science degrees train you in the latest practical technology skills—like software engineering, information security, algorithmic design, and data structures. More importantly, we teach you to think critically about technical issues so you can come up with creative solutions, no matter what industry you choose to work in.
Applied computer science majors are prepared for jobs as developers, analysts, and more in any industry you choose through in-depth courses in programming (including Python), web development, mobile apps, graphics, networks, communications, and more.
Program Highlights
Practical coursework that gets you career-ready—that’s the difference at USCA. We take theory and help you translate it into real-world application. Plus, you learn tools and techniques that enable you to address some of the digital world’s biggest questions confidently.
No matter which of our three Applied Computer Science degrees you choose, you can expect to:
- Pursue coursework that interests you. While our Applied Computer Science majors focus on one of three concentrations, we’re also a flexible major. We want you to take courses that meet your interests and prepare you for your future career. That’s why we offer a wide range of elective courses and minors to choose from, including data science, software engineering, and more.
- Learn—and be mentored by—highly experienced faculty members. Our computer science faculty members are adept educators, leading researchers, and experienced professionals in the field. They’ll be there to answer any questions you have throughout your studies and help you find applied computer science jobs that fit your interests and passions.
- Gain practical experience beyond the classroom. At USCA, internships with leading high-tech industries, capstone projects, and department presentations are just a few of the opportunities we offer to put your learning into action. At USCA, we prepare you for applied computer science jobs here in South Carolina and beyond.
“Careers in computer science are some of the fastest-growing professions in the world. At USCA, our faculty members partner with you to help you gain the skills to succeed in our ever-evolving high-tech world.”
