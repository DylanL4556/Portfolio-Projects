Writing Center Workshops are held each fall and spring semester. The workshop series is designed to assist students with writing across the disciplines. Some of your instructors may require attendance at one or more workshops throughout the semester. Some of your instructors may offer extra credit for attendance at one or more workshops throughout the semester.
Workshops are led by Writing Center consultants, professors, and administrators. They are designed to address various writing projects and common writing errors. The workshops are 30-50 minutes long and consist of a mix of lecture, discussion, and activity.
