Course Highlights
At USCA, you’ll take a combination of general education, core, and elective courses to prepare you for internships for English majors, your BA in English, a successful career in virtually any field, and graduate studies, should you wish to continue your education.
Our Senior Capstone course, for instance, allows you to take a graduate-level course and experience graduate-level work writing a thesis as an undergrad. Many of our English degree program graduates use their thesis as a writing sample for graduate school applications.
Other courses you may take will cover:
- American literature
- British literature
- Global/comparative literature
- English grammar
- Shakespeare
