I have to take the Mathematics Placement test. How do I access it?
Math Placement Tests are found in Blackboard.
Blackboard uses your USC Network Username and Password.
You should see them as soon as you log into Blackboard. If not, follow these instructions:
- Log into Blackboard at: https://blackboard.usca.edu
- If you are asked to complete a Blackboard profile go ahead and do so, or just click the "Do it later" link to continue into Blackboard.
- Once you are into Blackboard you'll see several modules. Find the "My Organizations" module on the right-side of the screen. You're looking for the "USC Aiken Math Placement Test" link highlighted in yellow in this example.
- Click the "USC Aiken Math Placement Test" and you should be immediately taken to the test.
