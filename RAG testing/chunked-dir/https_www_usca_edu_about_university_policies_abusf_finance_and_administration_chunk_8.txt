- Arrangements for on-campus food service/catering functions for internal campus groups should be made with ARAMARK at extension 3596. This includes coffee/danishes, cookies/soft drinks, or small luncheon arrangements.
- Arrangements for outside groups and all events held in the Conference Center should be made with Ms. Mary Anne Cavanaugh at extension 3587.
- Events should be arranged with as much advance notice as possible, a minimum of seven days is preferred. Events arranged within four working days of the event may be limited in menu options. ARAMARK will quote prices within 24 hours after the order is placed.
- For health and safety reasons, all leftover food items are the property of ARAMARK. We are unable to allow any leftover food to be taken off campus or to your offices. At the end of the event please contact the food services office so that the room may be cleared.
- A guaranteed minimum charge of $25 is required for all catered events. For catered functions under this minimum amount, there will be a $10 delivery/pick-up charge
- In the event of a cancellation within 48 hours of the event, the customer is responsible for the costs of any food and the production costs incurred up to the time of the cancellation. There will be no charges incurred if an event is cancelled with notice greater than 48 hours.
- Prior to ARAMARK finalizing plans for catering an event, the academic department/school or administrative unit must submit a purchase requisition to ARAMARK at least two days prior to the event.
- Submit a Campus Catering Ordering Form to ARAMARK (available on the Dining Services Web Site).
- Provide a final confirmed attendance or "guaranteed count" by 12:00 noon two business days prior to the event. The requesting department will be billed for the "guaranteed count" or actual number of people served, depending upon which number is greater.
- Pick Up Policy (Catering to Go)
- With assistance from ARAMARK, determine and order the appropriate quantity of food.
- ARAMARK will quote prices within 24 hours after the order is placed.
- The student organization or department must deliver a completed purchase requisition or check to ARAMARK at least 24 hours before the event.
