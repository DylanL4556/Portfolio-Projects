The Central Savannah River Area (CSRA) Regional Science & Engineering fair is associated with the The Intel® International Science and Engineering Fair® (Intel ISEF), the world's largest international pre-college science competition.
The event is sponsored by Savannah River Nuclear Solutions, and the Ruth Patrick Science Education Center.
