Accepting Awards
Once USCA has received your FAFSA and any verification requirements have been satisfied, the Financial Aid Counselors will assemble an aid package for you.
To view your award package, you must:
- Log into your Self Service Aiken (SSA) account
- Select the "Financial Aid"
- Select "Financial Aid Dashboard”
- Verify Aid Year in the top right corner
- Select the "Resources" tab
- Select the "Offer" tab on your financial aid dashboard to view your award offer
To accept or decline your awards:
- Log into your Self Service Aiken (SSA) account
- Select "Financial Aid"
- Select "Financial Aid Dashboard"
- Select the "Offer" tab to accept/decline award offer and submit
- You have three options to accept or decline awards including accepting the full amount of all awards, accepting or declining the full amount of each award individually, or accepting a partial amount of each award. For Federal Direct Loans, you must accept the full subsidized loan before you can accept the unsubsidized loan.
State Award Additional Requirements
Recipients of a South Carolina state scholarship (HOPE, LIFE, Palmetto Fellows) or South Carolina Need-based Grant must also sign a SC State Scholarship/Grant Certification before accepting the award. Your in-state residency must also be completed before a state award can be disbursed.
To sign your State Scholarship/Grant Certification:
- Log into Self Service Aiken (SSA)
- Select the “Financial Aid” tab
- Select “Financial Aid Dashboard”
- Select the "Offer" tab
- Accept the award offer and submit
To complete your SC residency:
- Log into Pacer "Admissions" Portal
- Review your Outstanding Requirements
- Click on your Residency Requirement to complete the process
Federal Direct Loan Additional Requirements
After accepting a Federal Direct Loan for the first time, you must complete Entrance Loan Counseling and sign a Master Promissory Note (MPN). You can complete the Entrance Loan Counseling and MPN at StudentAid.gov by signing into the site with your FSA ID.
