Exam Proctoring
If you have testing accommodations that cannot be handled within your classroom, exams can be proctored by Disability Services staff.
To schedule an exam, contact Sandra Robinson by phone at 803-643-6815 or email at sandrar@usca.edu or Dana Cullum by phone at 803-641-3609 or by email at danac@usca.edu.
- The professor must first have your Accommodation Form. These are provided to you by the Disability Services Coordinator once you have completed the registration process and orientation appointment.
- Coordinate accommodations with your professor, especially if you must take the exam at a different time from the class. (This can become an issue if your extended test-taking time interferes with another class or exam.)
- Schedule an exam with Disability Services staff at 803-643-6815 at least ONE (1) WEEK in advance.
Arrive in the office at least 10 minutes before your exam time. Turn off cell phones. Leave personal items outside of the testing area. - You must reschedule your appointment if you arrive 15 minutes or later after your exam time.
- Bring the supplies allowed/required by your professor, e.g., scratch paper, Scantron, calculator, pencils, and exam book. We do not supply Scantrons!
- You are not to leave the ATC for any reason other than an emergency once you begin your exam. Remember there might be other students taking exams at the same time as you. Taking an exam in the ATC is just like being in your classroom; the same rules apply.
- Arrange the method of handling any questions you may have with your professor ahead of time since they will most likely be administering the exam to the rest of the class simultaneously.
