Directions and Contact Information
The USCA Psychology Clinic is in the Robert E. Penland Administration Building on the University of South Carolina Aiken campus. Visit this page for an interactive map of the campus and to get directions. There is visitor parking available in the semi-circle driveway in front of the building. Take the elevator up to the second floor. Take a right off the elevator and walk straight down the hallway. Ring the doorbell and someone will let you in to the clinic.
