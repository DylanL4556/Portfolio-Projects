Family Celebration Brunch
10:30 a.m. the morning of graduation
The Celebration Brunch provides graduates, parents, family, and USC Aiken Faculty in the Convocation Center to celebrate this special day. The brunch will allow families and guests to view the Convocation Center, meet faculty and staff, and provide many photo opportunities.
Graduates receive a free Brunch ticket. Additional tickets are $15 per person ($5 for children ages 2-10). These tickets must be purchased from the Alumni Relations Office in the Alumni House.
Please purchase family tickets in advance using any of the below methods:
- Mail a check payable to Aiken Partnership and return the form to Alumni Relations - 471 University Parkway, Aiken, SC 29801.
- Please enclose the Senior/Family Celebration Brunch form
- Purchase your brunch tickets at the Alumni House on the USC Aiken campus; the address is 471 University Parkway, Aiken, South Carolina, 29801.
- Call the Office of Alumni Relations at 803-641-3480
