D. Appeals
Legitimate appeals may be submitted within 72 hours after issuance (Saturdays, Sundays, and holidays excepted); otherwise, the right to appeal is forfeited. Appeals will be presented before the Citation Appeals Committee for consideration. Each case is presented, and a final determination is made on the merit of the appeal and the infraction. Upon the committee's finding, a notification will be made via email as to the final results. The appeals form can be located in the University Police Department. You may download, print, and complete this form (USCA POLICE CITATION APPEAL) and bring it along with your original citation to the University Police Department.
