Other Giving Opportunities
The USCA Development staff is dedicated to making your giving experience meaningful and individualized. Please contact us for more information on gifts of appreciated assets, closely-held stock, real property, and planned giving. The Legacy Society at USCA entrusts that your planned gift through bequests, life insurance, gift annuities, charitable remainder trusts, charitable lead trusts, and IRA distributions will help secure the USCA legacy.
You can make your gift to USCA online through the online giving site! We accept all major credit cards, including American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa. You may also designate your gift to specific areas at USCA, and you can give in memory or honor of someone.
