In most cases, if left alone, the update process will complete within about 10-minutes. However, if you have previously forced the process to stop by rebooting or powering-off, it will likely take 30-45 minutes to complete the update process. This is because Windows will not only have to complete the update, but first repair the errors caused by the forced stop on its previous attempt. If your Windows system does have a black screen with only a cursor, and it remains this way for longer then 45-minutes, please do not power your system off until instructed to do so.
