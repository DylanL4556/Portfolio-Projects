USCA honors its graduates with a special ceremony held in December and May each year.
Congratulations to all of our graduates! This is a special time for you and your families to celebrate. This information is provided to make the day more enjoyable.
Tickets for family and friends are not required for the December ceremony.
Graduate tickets are required to enter the Convocation Center.
Date and Time: December 11, 2025, at 6:00 p.m. Graduates must be at the Convocation Center no later than 5:00 p.m. We strongly encourage everyone to attend the rehearsal/information session at 11:30 a.m. on December 11 in the Convocation Center.
Location: USCA Convocation Center, 2049 Champion Way, Graniteville, SC 29829. This is across the Robert Bell Parkway from the main campus.
Tickets: Graduates can pick up their tickets at the Grad Finale or the Alumni House.
