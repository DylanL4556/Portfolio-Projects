Transfer and Non-Traditional Students are students who 1) are transferring to USCA from another institution with more than 15 credit hours; 2) have attended another USC system campus and earned more than 15 credit hours; or 3) have been out of high school for at least five years.
Transfer students must attend an orientation program to facilitate a smooth transition to USCA.
You will be introduced to faculty, staff, and students during the orientation program. You’ll learn about your opportunities and responsibilities as a student. During orientation, you will meet with your academic advisor, become informed about your academic program and degree requirements, and register for classes.
