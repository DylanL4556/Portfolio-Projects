Membership Renewal Policies
- Members must present proof of affiliation each time they join or renew their membership.
- New and renewing members not in the computer system must complete the Medical History and Informed Consent.
- In the affiliated category, the spouse of the affiliate can only become a member if the original affiliated person is a member. (I.e., Alumni spouse cannot join unless the alumni is a member)
* Memberships cannot be canceled or extended except for extenuating circumstances once purchased. The director must approve any cancellations or extensions which will be made on a case-by-case basis.
