Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
USCA accepts applications on a rolling basis throughout the year. It’s recommended that students in their final year of high school complete the application for admission during the fall to allow plenty of time to process it. Once we have all pieces of your application of admissions (application, transcript, and fee), we will work diligently to determine your admissions status.
Ensure you have submitted all material for admission (application fee, high school transcript). You can call the Office of Admissions at (803) 641-3366 to check your status.
Make sure to apply and be admitted before February 1 of your senior year to qualify for our merit-based scholarships potentially. Scholarships are awarded based on a combination of test scores (optional but preferred for scholarship consideration), GPA, and class rank.
