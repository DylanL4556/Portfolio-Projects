Our Programs:
Education Program at Sumter
USCA has partnered with USC Sumter to offer Bachelor’s Degrees in Early Childhood and Elementary Education to students in the Sumter area. The Commission on Higher Education approved the entire program to be jointly offered on the USC Sumter campus. USCA and Sumter County Schools entered a Professional Development School partnership where our candidates at USC Sumter and faculty interact with PK-12 students in a high-needs area.
