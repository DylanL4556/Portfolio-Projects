Alumni and Student Engagement Committee
The Alumni and Engagement Committee provides alumni and current students opportunities to network and become part of the BAC. You may be asked to attend monthly or bi-monthly meetings and participate in weekend or after-work events.

Marketing and Communication Committee
The Marketing and Communication Committee is responsible for all BAC marketing and communications to the membership and general public. BAC Marketing and Communication is the first and official source for information regarding events, fundraising initiatives, alumni awareness, etc. You may be asked to meet regularly, design marketing materials, and brainstorm ideas to promote BAC.
If you are interested in serving on the Marketing and Communications Committee, please contact the Vice-Chair of Marketing and Communications, Jasmine Preston at Jasmine.Preston@usca.edu, for more information.
