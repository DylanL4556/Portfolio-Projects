Course Highlights
The major in Spanish at USCA is designed to give you mastery of the language, as well as the cultural knowledge to communicate effectively and fluently. Spanish majors take core classes to learn advanced vocabulary and grammar, become comfortable in conversation, and use the language in the real world.
Choose the concentration that caters to your interests
Are you interested in diving into the nuances of the Spanish language, the history of the Spanish-speaking world, or the diverse literature it has produced? If so, the concentration in Spanish Language, Literatures, and Cultures may be the right track for you.
Through this track, you will enrich your understanding of Spanish language, literature, and culture with courses in:
- Latin American, Peninsular/Iberian, and Caribbean literature
- The civilizations and cultures of Latin America and the Iberian Peninsula
- The histories of the Spanish-speaking world
For those wanting to pursue a career in Spanish-English Translation & Interpretation, this track prepares you for a variety of interesting jobs. You will learn techniques to help you bridge the gap between English and Spanish speakers through courses that delve into the theory and practice behind interpreting and translating.
This track includes courses that explore a variety of professions, including:
- Education
- Medical
- Business & Finance
- Public Service
