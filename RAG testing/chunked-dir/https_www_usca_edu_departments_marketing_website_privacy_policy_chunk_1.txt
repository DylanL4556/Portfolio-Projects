This Privacy Statement is designed to provide information about how USCA collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from its users (each, a “User”) of the www.usca.edu website (“Site”). We respect and value the privacy of our users and understand the importance of protecting their personal information. For this reason, we take appropriate measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of their information.
Personally-identifiable information
We may ask users for personal information when they visit our site, register, subscribe to a newsletter, respond to a survey, fill out a form, or use other site features. “Personal information” means information about you such as your name, email, or other identifiers related to you or can be related to. Users may keep this information private, although it may limit their ability to use certain site features.
Non-personally identifiable information
We might gather information that doesn’t identify users when they use our site. This information may include the browser being used, their computer type, and technical details about how they’re connected to our site, such as the operating system and internet service provider they’re using.
Web browser cookies
Our website might use “cookies” to make your experience better. A cookie is a small file our website saves on your computer to remember browsing habits. Users may stop cookies or get a notification when cookies are being sent, but doing so may affect the site's functionality.
How we use collected information
USCA may use your personal information to respond more efficiently to your requests and support needs and improve your site experience. We use information to understand how our users interact with our site and to improve our products and services.
How we protect your information
We take steps to collect, store, and use your personal information safely on our website. We also have security measures in place to protect your information from being accessed, changed, shared, or destroyed without your permission.
Sharing your personal information
We do not share or sell your personal information to anyone. We may also use other companies to help us run our business, like sending out newsletters or surveys. If we share your information with these companies, we will only do so with your permission and for a specific purpose.
Public Records
Information collected from users may be subject to inspection and copying by the public, as required by South Carolina laws.
