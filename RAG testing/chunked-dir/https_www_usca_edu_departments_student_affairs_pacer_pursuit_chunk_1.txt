What is the Pacer Pursuit?
Pacer Pursuit is the ultimate way for students to experience USCA. We have identified 63 traditions we believe provide that experience. The Pacer Pursuit works by attending one of the traditions and taking a selfie of yourself there. Once you have submitted all of your pictures, a committee of faculty/staff will review your submission to receive your reward—a medal to wear at your graduation! You must submit 45 of the 63 traditions listed to complete the Pacer Pursuit.
How to Participate
Take a picture of yourself at one of the traditions listed on Presence and upload the picture to the Pacer Pursuit Experience. We will keep track of all of your photos.
Deadline
All pictures must be uploaded ten days before graduation for seniors graduating in any semester.
