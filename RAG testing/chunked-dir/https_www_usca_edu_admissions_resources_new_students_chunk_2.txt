3. Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
If you plan to fund your education using Financial Aid, submitting your FAFSA early can be crucial to be considered. The new FAFSA is available every year beginning January 1. The USCA Financial Aid school code is 003449. Students entering USCA for the Fall semester will receive their financial aid offer (assuming FAFSA has been submitted and approved) online via Self-Service Aiken beginning in early April.
