Online Palmetto College
USCA is proud to provide seven bachelor’s degree programs online in Business Administration, Communication, Cybersecurity, Emerging Media, Liberal Studies, Psychology and, Special Education through the USC Palmetto College.
Application deadlines for Palmetto College are July 15 for the Fall semester and December 1 for the Spring semester. Any applications submitted after these dates will automatically be rolled over for consideration for the next available semester. All students interested in Palmetto College should consult with an advisor in order to determine eligibility.
