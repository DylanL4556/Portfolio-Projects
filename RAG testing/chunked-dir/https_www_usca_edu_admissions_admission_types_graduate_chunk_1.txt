At the University of South Carolina Aiken, we are committed to fostering an environment that encourages innovation, critical thinking, and personal growth. Our graduate programs are designed to challenge and inspire, leading you to professional excellence and leadership.
We offer several graduate programs, each designed to meet the unique needs of our students. Our graduate programs are structured to provide a comprehensive and engaging learning experience.
Why Choose USCA for Your Graduate Studies?
- Personalized Attention: Benefit from small class sizes and dedicated faculty who are experts in their fields and genuinely invested in your success.
- Research Opportunities: Engage in cutting-edge research that pushes boundaries and contributes to real-world solutions.
- Career Advancement: Our programs are tailored to meet the demands of the job market, equipping you with the skills and knowledge to excel in your career.
- Community Engagement: Immerse yourself in a campus culture that values service and leadership, offering numerous opportunities to impact our community positively.
- Affordable Excellence: Enjoy high-quality education at a competitive cost, with various financial aid options available to support your journey.
Requirements
All graduate applicants must submit the following items:
- Graduate application with a $45 application fee
- Official transcripts from all universities attended (Form)
- Three (3) letters of recommendation (Form)
- Proof of immunization (Form)
- Proof of citizenship (Form)
Official transcripts are sent directly from your institution to Graduate Admissions or submitted as a hardcopy in an envelope sealed by your institution. Transcripts sent by the student are not considered official. Electronic copies sent by the student are also not official. Please have electronic transcripts sent directly by the university to enroll@usca.edu.
If you have any questions about graduate admissions, please email enroll@usca.edu. Any documents (official transcripts, resumes, letters of recommendation, statements of purpose) can be emailed to the same email address.
