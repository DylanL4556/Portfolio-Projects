Business Cards and Letterhead
The university has a storefront account set up with an established university supplier.
Each department should appoint one individual to order stationery for everyone within that department. You must request an account from the Director of Marketing to get started. Once your account is created, you will receive complete instructions on ordering your letterhead, envelopes, and business cards.
Digital Signage and Marquee
Have an event, meeting, or club to promote? Submit a slide image (jpg or png) in 16:9 ratio (landscape) to fit the ratio of a television screen for best display legibility. Do not submit PDFs. If you submit a flyer (8.5 x 11), it will be returned to you.
The "marquee" refers to the electronic sign located on University Parkway (parking lot A). The marquee is limited to text.
Submit your content for digital signage or the marquee via email to website@usca.edu.

Report Inappropriate Content
Occasionally, abusive accounts emerge on social media platforms. The university cannot directly manage or eliminate the content on other user accounts. Nevertheless, if you come across unsuitable content online, there are measures you can implement to notify the platform, which could result in the content's removal. Should you encounter any abusive content, we urge you to report it and share the link with us at socialmedia@usca.edu. This will enable us to include it in our records of abusive accounts.
Photography Services
University Marketing and Communication handles the photography needs for major university events (excluding Athletics) and the Office of the Chancellor. These events include the following:
- Graduation ceremonies (December and May)
- Academic Honors
- Strategic photoshoots (to capture assets for recruitment purposes)
Our office is unable to coordinate photography for any other event. You will need to book and pay for a freelance photographer (see recommendations below) or use your smartphone to capture your event (see tips for taking great photos), which is ideal for use on social media platforms.
