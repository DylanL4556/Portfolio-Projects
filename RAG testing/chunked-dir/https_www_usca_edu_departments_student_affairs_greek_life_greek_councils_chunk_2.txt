
The National Pan-Hellenic Council at the USCA, known collectively as the NPHC, does hereby embrace the ideals of unity, achievement, and service to the community. We strive to manifest the fellowship of our respective organizations in everything we do, to inspire this idea of fellowship in every individual we encounter, to empower our people socially, economically, and politically through education, and to unite our campus community through positive communication and relation.
The NPHC Council, more commonly called the “Divine 9,” comprises historically (not exclusively) African American fraternities and sororities. USCA currently houses six of the nine groups: Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc., Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc., Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc., and Sigma Gamma Rho Sorority, Inc. Women and men interested in an NPHC organization should connect with the members within the organization they are interested in, or you can fill out the interest form to get connected.
