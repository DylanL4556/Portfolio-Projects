Find Your Scholarship
Whether you are an incoming freshman, a dedicated continuing student, or a transfer student looking to join our community, we have curated a wide range of scholarship opportunities to help ease the financial burden of your educational journey. Below, you’ll find direct links to search for scholarships that best match your academic achievements, financial needs, and personal interests.
USCA Scholarships
At USCA, we are committed to recognizing and rewarding academic excellence through our institutional academic scholarships. These scholarships are designed to support students who have demonstrated outstanding academic achievement, leadership qualities, and a commitment to their community.
Depending on the academic department, awards vary in amount and may have special criteria to apply. Scholarships in these categories do require an application:
- Alumni Scholarships
- College of Sciences and Engineering WORC Scholarship
- Department of Biology & Geology Scholarships
- Department of English Writing Awards & Scholarship
- Department of Visual & Performing Arts Scholarships
- Office of International Programs Scholarships
- School of Education Scholarships
- School of Nursing Honors, Awards, and Scholarships (HAS)
- Veteran and Military Student Success Scholarships
The university does have a number of scholarship opportunities for incoming freshman (such as the University Merit Awards) and transfer students, plus other scholarships that do not require an application.
For more information on the current available university scholarships for this academic year, and the criteria (if any) to apply, contact the Office of Financial Aid at (803) 641-3476 or stuaid@usca.edu.
South Carolina Scholarships
The state of South Carolina offers a range of need-based and merit-based scholarships and grant opportunities for state resident students to support their educational goals. These unique state-sponsored scholarships ensure resident students can pursue their dreams without financial barriers.
Some examples of state-sponsored scholarships include:
- SC HOPE
- Life Scholarship
- Palmetto Fellows
