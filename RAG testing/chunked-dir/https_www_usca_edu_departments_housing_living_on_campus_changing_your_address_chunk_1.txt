Pacer Crossings Residents:
Your Name
961 Leadership Drive and your Suite Number and Bedroom Letter
Aiken, SC 29801
Pacer Commons Residents:
Your Name
855 Leadership Drive and your Apartment Number
Aiken, SC 29801
Pacer Downs Residents:
Your Name
Unit # Leadership Drive and Your Apartment Letter (Located on Front Door)
*For example: 807 Leadership Drive Apt. A
Aiken, SC 29801
Mail sent to 471 University Parkway, Aiken, SC 29801, will not be delivered to you, as Housing mail does not go through the central USCA mail room.
**If you change your address in Self Service Aiken to your on-campus address at the end of the semester, change it back to your permanent address.
The United States Postal Service does not forward mail; therefore, mail will be returned to the sender. When you change your mailing address in Self Service Aiken, all your USCA documents (including financial aid letters, etc.) will be delivered on time.
