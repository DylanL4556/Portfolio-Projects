Course Highlights
The sports science degree curriculum includes courses in athletic training, specific sports, and core courses to give you the skills and knowledge to become a professional in your chosen area. As an exercise science major, you will also take the general education coursework required for your bachelor of exercise science degree. Classes include:
- Fitness Assessment and Exercise Leadership
- Human Anatomy and Physiology
- Health and Behavior Change
- Electrocardiography
- Exercise Prescription
Pick a concentration for your sports science degree
You can choose a focus for your bachelor of exercise science from one of the following options:
- Applied Exercise Science
- Allied Health
- Applied Exercise Science Pre-Physical Therapy
- Applied Exercise Science Pre-Occupational Therapy
What can you do with an Exercise & Sports Science degree?
Our bachelor of exercise science degree is excellent preparation for many career options. The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects that jobs for fitness instructors will grow by 19% and some allied health specialties by 25% over the next 10 years, which is much faster than average.
