Ordering Branded Merchandise & Collateral – Quick Reference Guide
University Marketing & Communication often receives questions about where to order branded materials. This guide will help you quickly find the right vendor, process, and resources.
Merchandise (swag, apparel, promotional items)
University Approved Storefront: Showpony
Other options:
University Bookstore – Available online and in person. Faculty & staff receive a 10% discount.
Approved Vendors – Other approved vendors may be used. Please provide them with the Brand Guidelines (download below). For first-time orders, the Marketing Department requests to review proofs before production.
Embroidery
Innovative Solutions has our design files on hand and follows brand guidelines. You may purchase clothing elsewhere and bring it to them for embroidery.
Other embroidery vendors may be used, but please confirm they are familiar with and adhere to our brand guidelines.
Note: Always confirm brand compliance before placing orders.
