The USCA Alumni Association serves more than 16,000 alumni and seeks to have a board that reflects the diversity within the alumni body. The one common trait of all candidates will be their ability to advance USCA by engaging all alumni.
The Alumni Association
The USCA Alumni Association serves all university alumni. The association aims to advance USCA’s best interests and well-being by engaging all alumni in the institution’s life through communication, programming, services, and volunteer opportunities.
About the Alumni Board
The Alumni Board is an advisory group for and represents the Alumni Association. It is comprised of 15 members who must be active members (contributions of at least $25 made during the current fiscal year) and shall serve two-year terms. The Board will have at least one representative from each of the two colleges and from each of the three professional schools. Members will be selected by the Executive Committee of the Alumni Board.
