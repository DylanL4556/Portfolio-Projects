Facilities
Space is available for lunch upon request and availability. Groups must bring their own food and drinks. The RPSEC does not provide lunch for groups.
ADA Statement
Please indicate if you need any special services, assistance, or accommodations to participate in our programs by contacting us in advance at rpsec@usca.edu or (803) 641-3313.
Reservation Request Information
Ideally, request forms should be submitted at least two (2) weeks in advance. After you submit a reservation request, you will receive an e-mail confirmation letter with details about your scheduled visit. Signed confirmation letters must be returned at least one (1) week before your visit.
Questions
If you have any questions regarding your visit, please call (803) 641-3313.
