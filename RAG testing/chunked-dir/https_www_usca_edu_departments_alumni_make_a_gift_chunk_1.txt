Upon making a gift of $25 or more to any program or university department whose private funds are administered through the Aiken Partnership Board, an alum is considered a member of the USCA Alumni Association. There are no additional dues.
Learn more about the USC Aiken Alumni Association
How to Give
Thank you in advance for your support! You can make your gift to USCA online through the USCA Online Giving site. The website accepts all major credit cards, including American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa. You may also designate your gift to specific areas at USCA.
Alumni who wish to give directly to the Alumni Office or the Alumni Association should indicate that they wish to give to the Alumni Development Fund or Alumni Scholarship Funds.
After entering your gift amount on the first page, be sure "USCA" is listed under your gift designation on the second page.
For more information on the ways to give and the ways you can designate your gift, please contact the University Advancement Office at 803-641-3408.
Benefits to Giving
Once alumni have given to the area of their choice, they will receive an alumni card in the mail, which includes the following Pacer Privileges:
- Subscription to the university magazine Connections
- Subscription to the alumni E-Newsletter
- Invitations to alumni functions
- Advance notice of USCA programs, conferences & special events
- Membership card
- Voting and office-holding privileges in the Association
- Use of Wellness Center and Natatorium (with additional fee)
For Information on the USCA Alumni Association, call 803-641-3480 or email alumni@usca.edu.
