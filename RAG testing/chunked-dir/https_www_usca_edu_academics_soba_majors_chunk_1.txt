Our comprehensive suite of programs, ranging from undergraduate degrees in the areas of Accounting, Finance, Management, and Marketing, to our innovative MBA program, is designed to equip students with the skills necessary to excel in the dynamic world of business.
Online Program Options
Palmetto College is a 100% online program where courses are offered asynchronously through Blackboard. Our business curriculum has been developed to reflect today's employers' real-world needs, so you'll graduate with the problem-solving skills that will get you hired and promoted in the modern marketplace.
All School of Business Administration concentrations can be completed fully online through the Palmetto College transfer program or by starting as a USCA traditional student. Students can also choose to take a blend of online and traditional courses if desired.
Business Program at Sumter
The Aiken Business Program at Sumter allows USC Sumter students to earn an AACSB-accredited BSBA degree from USCA without relocating. This established program, which graduates around twenty students annually, is accessible after fulfilling lower-level requirements at USC Sumter. Eligible students can complete their degrees through a mix of in-person classes at USC Sumter and online courses led by USCA SOBA faculty.
