Graduate Studies Scholarship (GSS)
The MCIS Graduate Studies Scholarship (GSS) is designed to support high-potential graduate students with financial aid and tuition benefits:
- Receive up to $500 annually (renewable based on academic performance)
- Extends in-state tuition eligibility to on-campus non-resident and international students
