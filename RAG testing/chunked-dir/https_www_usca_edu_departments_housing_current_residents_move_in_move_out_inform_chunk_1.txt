Spring 2026 New Resident Move In Information
Once you complete your University Housing Application, including the payment of the Housing Application Fee and the Housing Security Deposit, you will receive an email updating you on your ability to move in.
Move-In will take place on Sunday, Jan. 11, 2026, between 1:00 pm and 5:00 pm. You will be able to pick up your keys at the desk of the building that you are moving into for the spring semester.
Those residents who have signed up for January 8, 2026 Orientation will be able to move into University Housing on Wednesday, January 7, 2026, between 3:00 pm to 4:30 pm. Keys will be available in Housing Office at Pacer Crossings 130. Please complete the Early Arrival Form so University Housing is aware that you will be arriving to move in for Orientation on January 7.
Early arrival dates are only granted for university related activities, such as participation in orientation, attending a training session or working in a university office. Please complete your request on the form below.
The deadline to submit this form is Friday January 2, 2026.
Please know that University Housing will verify that you are an approved participant in a university sponsored event that would require your early arrival for Spring 2026. All residents who are approved for early arrival will be notified at the email they have provided on this form no later than Tuesday January 6, 2026. All approved residents will be provided information on the check in date, time, and processes in that email.
New resident will go to the desk of the building that you have your room assignment in for the spring semester. For instance, if you are moving into Pacer Crossings, then you will go to the Pacer Crossings desk. Resident Mentors will be able to assist you with checking in and getting your keys.
Please make sure to check your student housing portal for the building name and room number you will be staying in before arriving to campus. You may also call 803-641-3790 if you are unable to login.
