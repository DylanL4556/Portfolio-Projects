Our language immersion program
Our program is designed for USCA students to earn 3 hours of transfer credit for Spanish 102, Spanish 210, or Spanish 316/317. Courses are taught by professors from Universitas Castellae in Valladolid, a language institution receiving Study Abroad students for over 25 years.
Students will live with host families to further improve their Spanish.
Valladolid
History
Located alongside the Pisuerga River and only 2 hours from Madrid, Valladolid has seen Roman, Islamic, and Christian civilizations
Culture
With countless museums, plazas, churches, and cultural venues, Valladolid offers daily entertainment
Food
Tapas are especially popular in the Castilian capital. Bars and restaurants offer unlimited options to taste the Spanish cuisine
