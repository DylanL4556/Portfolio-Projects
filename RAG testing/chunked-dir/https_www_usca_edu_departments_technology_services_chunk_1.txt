The Technology Services Division (TSD) is here to help students, faculty, and staff with all their technology needs. From computing to networking, instructional technology, and voice communication tools, we’ve got you covered.
Explore our range of services designed to elevate your experience:
-
Technical Support: Whether you're troubleshooting a software glitch or seeking guidance on hardware configurations, our knowledgeable team at the Technology Services Help Desk is here to help. Our Student Help Desk is located on the first floor of the Gregg-Graniteville Library, and our Faculty/Staff Help Desk is located in B&E 100.
-
Network Infrastructure: Stay connected effortlessly with our robust network infrastructure, ensuring reliable access to online resources and seamless communication.
-
Instructional Technology: Enhance teaching and learning experiences with cutting-edge tools and resources tailored to support diverse instructional needs.
-
Voice Communication: Streamline your communication channels with our efficient voice communication solutions, facilitating collaboration and connectivity across the campus community.
USCA's Technology Services Division is committed to providing a reliable, state-of-the-art campus technology environment.
