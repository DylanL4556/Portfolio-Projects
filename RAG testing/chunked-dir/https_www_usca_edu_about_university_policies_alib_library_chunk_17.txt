 Reference, instruction, and special circulation services to the outside community can be extended only when they do not interfere with services to the campus community. In some cases the Library may provide certain services to external or contractual users on a cost-recovery basis.
All access and services are subject to restrictions at periods of high use by primary clientele, or by the need to conserve or protect Library materials or equipment.
