Welcome to the Department of Sociology at the University of South Carolina Aiken (USCA), where we explore the intricate web of human society, culture, and behavior to understand and address the pressing social issues of our time.
Sociology is a dynamic and interdisciplinary field that offers a unique lens through which to examine the complexities of human interaction, social structures, and global trends. Our department provides students with a comprehensive education that combines theoretical insights with practical skills, preparing them to navigate diverse social landscapes and effect positive change in their communities.
Whether you're interested in pursuing a career in social work, criminal justice, public policy, or community advocacy, the Department of Sociology at USCA provides the foundation you need to make a meaningful impact in the world. Join us in examining the social forces that shape our lives, challenging assumptions, and working towards a more just and equitable society.
