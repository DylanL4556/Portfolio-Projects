Students receive job training by completing two additional USCA LIFE-specific courses: Job Training I and II. In these courses, students gain the knowledge and skills to find, obtain, and maintain employment.
Students work about 5-20 hours per week each semester (with support from staff). During the first year, students complete an unpaid internship on campus. During the second year, students work within the campus and local communities.
