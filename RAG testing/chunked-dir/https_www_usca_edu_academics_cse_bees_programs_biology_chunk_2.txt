Degree Course Highlights
Whether you’re pursuing our Bachelor of Arts in Biology or Bachelor of Science in Biology, you’ll experience a breadth of coursework, labs, and research across a variety of scientific fields.
Biology majors can expect coursework in:
- Genetics
- Ecology
- Evolution
- Biostatistics
- Integrated Systems
- Organismal Biology
- Cellular and Molecular Biology
All biology majors also take part in a senior seminar and a 12-month research project under the mentorship of a biology faculty member.
For more information about the Biology Program, visit our department page and be sure to check out the Student Handbook on our Resources page.

What can you do with a Biology degree?
Graduates of USCA’s Bachelor’s degree in Biology are prepared to enter careers requiring a high-quality education—or move on to graduate or professional schools—in areas such as health and wellness, industry and nonprofits, marine and wildlife resources, teaching, and more.
