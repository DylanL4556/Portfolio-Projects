Goal
The University Police Department’s goal is to provide each community member with the most effective service and protection within our means. Therefore, it is essential that the community we serve has faith in us and is aware of all that its police department has to offer.
Alerts
The university uses your emergency notification information to alert you to real-time life-threatening events and unscheduled campus closures. You should keep this information up-to-date.
You’ll be prompted once each major semester to update your information the first time you log into SSC. However, you may also update your information at any time.
