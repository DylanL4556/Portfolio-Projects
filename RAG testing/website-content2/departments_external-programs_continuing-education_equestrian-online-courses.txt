Equestrian Online Courses
Welcome to USCA's equestrian online courses. We offer 24 different horse-related classes. You may begin your online, non-credit course(s) at any point in the month; take one course or several at a time. The completion deadline is one year from the registration date of each individual course. Click on the course titles below to read about the course content and view the syllabus.
