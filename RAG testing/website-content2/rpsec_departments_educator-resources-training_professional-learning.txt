Providing professional learning activities for teachers is a primary method for reaching our goal of “Infusing a Love for Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics.” The primary way of impacting student achievement is through highly qualified teachers. Professional Learning at the RPSEC offers a variety of activities during the summer and the academic year. Please contact us at RPSEC@usca.edu or 803-641-3313 if you would like to arrange professional learning activities for your school or district.
