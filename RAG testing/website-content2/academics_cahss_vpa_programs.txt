We offer degrees in art (concentrations in art history, graphic design, and studio and digital art), music (concentrations in instrumental, piano, and vocal studies), K-12 teacher certification in choral or instrumental music), and theatre and liberal studies. Students may also minor in studio art, art history, music, and theatre.
