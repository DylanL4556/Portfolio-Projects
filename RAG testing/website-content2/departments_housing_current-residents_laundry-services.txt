Laundry Services | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/housing/current-residents/laundry-services/
USCA Housing laundry rooms are located in each housing building for the use of our housing residents. A washer load costs $1.75 (lasting 35-40 minutes) and a dryer load costs $1.50 (lasting 60 minutes). There are two ways to pay for the washers and dryers, by using quarters or through the SpeedQueen App.
Getting Quarters on Campus
There is a change machine located in the SAC, near the pool tables, and it is able to make change for you to use in the washers and dryers.
Using the SpeedQueen App
The SpeedQueen App is available for both Apple and Android phones. Search for the SpeedQueen App and download it to your phone. Once you have it installed, then set it up for USCA, and use your USCA email to create an account.
Through the SpeedQueen App, payments may be made with credit and debit cards. This is then able to be used to directly select the washers and dyers in each of the laundry rooms. The App also allows the user to select the load types and get text and other alerts as to when the load is almost done (a 3 minute warning) and completed.
Report an Issue
The university contracts with Caldwell & Gregory for laundry services. If you have a problem with one of their machines, please submit a work order by calling Caldwell and Gregory at 1-800-927-9274, or you can download the app, scan the QR Code on the machine, and send it directly to Caldwell & Gregory.
