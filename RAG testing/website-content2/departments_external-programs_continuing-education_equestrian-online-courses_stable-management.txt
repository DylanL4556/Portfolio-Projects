Stable Management
Course Description
This course is designed to provide the student with the skills to properly care for horses in a training, boarding, or breeding facility.
Starting with a review of horse knowledge and the basics of proper horse handling, the student will learn to observe all the conditions that affect the horse, from health care to equipment choice and maintenance.
Students will learn to use different restraints, give intermuscular injections, and administer oral medications. They will also examine alternative healing therapies.
You need a video camera and the ability to load videos to a video hosting website such as YouTube. You must also have access to a horse.
