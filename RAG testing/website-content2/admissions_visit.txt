Visit USCA
Join us at USCA for an Admissions presentation and a 75-minute campus tour led by a student ambassador. Explore housing, dining, classrooms, and more. You always have the chance to speak with a staff member post tour about next steps. It is a walking tour so dress for the weather and wear comfortable shoes.
