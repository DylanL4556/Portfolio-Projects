Call for Submissions
We invite scholars, professors, instructors, and researchers to submit abstracts for the second annual conference “Languages in the South.”
This conference aims to examine the state of the field of language teaching, learning, and education from various perspectives with a particular focus on institutions in the Southeastern United States.
We encourage proposals for 15-minute individual presentations that address, but are not limited to, the following:
- Experiential learning
- Acquisition and linguistics
- Technology and its impacts
- Sign language
- English as a Second Language
- K-12 education
- Languages in the workforce
- Recruitment and retention
- Partnerships and internships
- Career preparedness/development
- Issues in administration
- Digital/applied humanities
Abstracts
Abstracts should be a PDF document of no more than 150 words.
Please include a 75-word biography.
Send abstracts to Christopher Botero (cbotero@augusta.edu)
and Ángel M. Rańales (angel.ranales@usca.edu).
