For more information on giving and designating your gift, please get in touch with the University Advancement Office at (803) 641-3448 or email us.
You can make your gift to USC Aiken online through our online giving site. We accept all major credit cards, including American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa. You may also designate your gift to specific areas at USCA, and you can give in memory or honor of someone.
