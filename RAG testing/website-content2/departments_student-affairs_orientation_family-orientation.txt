Family Orientation | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/student-affairs/orientation/family-orientation/
Parents, guardians, family members, and guests can register for a parent/family program when students register for their orientation programs. Parents and family play a huge role in their student’s development and education. During the parent/family program, you will obtain information about our university and gain insight into the adjustments and expectations for your students as they begin their college careers at USCA.
The program will cover student involvement, financial aid, student healthcare, campus safety, support services, housing, and other topics that will help your student succeed at USCA. Due to the length and material covered, the program is unsuitable for younger children.
After morning check-in, parents and students will have separate sessions for much of the day as students meet classmates and advisors.
The parent/guest orientation is optional.
We hope you can participate in our orientation and look forward to seeing you and your student on campus this summer.
