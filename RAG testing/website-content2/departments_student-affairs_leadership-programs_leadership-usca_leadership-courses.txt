Below is a list of leadership courses currently offered at USCA:
Emerging Leaders Class (ASUP 110)
2 credit hours
The Emerging Leaders class is a two-credit course that includes a weekend retreat, a service learning requirement, group leadership projects, readings, and participation in campus life activities. The class is an excellent way for students to improve and strengthen their leadership skills.
Citizen Leadership (ASUP 210)
2 credit hours
Citizen Leadership Is a two-credit course that focuses on applying leadership theory to active service learning experiences. Students will look at citizenship from various perspectives and reflect on their roles as leaders and citizens. (Prereq: 30 hours)
Leadership Exchange (ASUP 310)
2 credit hours
This is a unique leadership opportunity in which USCA is paired with another university in the country to examine a social issue facing our world. USCA students will spend four days at the other university, and then their students will spend four days at USCA.
Senior Year Experience (ASUP 410)
1 credit hour
This class will help you tie together all of your college experiences to make you as marketable as possible in the new world and job market while focusing on being an active citizen in your community.
