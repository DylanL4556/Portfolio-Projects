We welcome students to USCA and invite them to share in our successes. We want our students to exhibit and promote civility, respect for others, integrity, honesty, and generally lead by example.
In line with this, USCA has instituted both an Academic Code of Conduct and a Non-Academic Code of Conduct to handle situations that might arise.
