Who is it for?
The Education Alumni Chapter welcomes any USCA alum who works in the field of education.
Our mission
The mission of the Education Alumni Chapter is to celebrate, advance, and support USCA Graduates working in the profession of education. To fulfill this mission, we will:
- Recognize impactful contributions
- Recruit and retain high quality educators
- Provide networking and professional development opportunities
When do we meet?
We have a virtual meeting every other month.
