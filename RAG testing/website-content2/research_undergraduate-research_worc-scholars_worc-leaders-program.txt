WORC Leaders Program
What is the WORC Leaders Program?
The WORC Leaders Program is a leadership program aimed at producing excellent STEM graduates who are also accomplished and experienced leaders. This is a unique component of the WORC Scholars Program.
Exceptional engineering, mathematics, computer science, chemistry, biology (ERRP), and earth systems science students will be selected as freshmen by a competitive process. Each recipient will receive a scholarship in their freshman year that may be renewed each year while in college (based on GPA, continuation in STEM major, and meeting requirements of WORC Leaders Program listed below yearly). The scholarship is offered through the generosity of the Savannah River Site Community Reuse Organization.
WORC Leaders will:
- Serve as emcees at WORC-sponsored oral sessions of Scholar Showcase
- Attend and promote STEM at the STEM Career Connections Fair
- Attend and promote STEM Friday
- Attend and promote our college's “Science on Tap” and “STEM in the Alley” events yearly. Both are scientific outreach events hosted in downtown Aiken to celebrate science and student research. These events involve a student poster session and two research presentations by USCA faculty (or SRNL staff scientists for the “Science on Tap” events). These events provide food and fellowship for participants and build relationships that lead to collaborations.
- “WORC Leaders” will complete USCA’s Leadership Certificate, which involves leadership coursework and has service-learning components. (Must complete ASUP 110 in freshman year, ASUP 210 in sophomore year, and ASUP 310 in junior or senior year)
- “WORC Leaders” will be assigned an SRNL University Affiliate as their key mentor (part of the existing MOU with SRNL).
- We currently have University Affiliates in Materials Science/Geology and Chemistry.
