Convocation Center | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/rentals/convocation-center/
The Convocation Center is a state-of-the-art facility with a capacity ranging from 3,500 to 4,100 patrons.
It is located on the beautiful University of South Carolina Aiken Campus. It features a first-class VIP Room with a capacity of up to 200 patrons. It also features multiple electronic scoreboards with backlighting advertisement opportunities, rentable meeting space for any function, and two additional practice courts.
The University of South Carolina Aiken is the primary owner and operator of the Convocation Center.
Facility Specifics
- 97,600 Sq. ft of total space (including rentable meeting space)
- Retractable seating to accommodate up to 4,000 attendees
- Multiple locker rooms to accommodate athletic events, concerts, and other requirements
- Main floor used for USCA athletic events, concerts, graduations, and other public events
- Two additional practice courts, which can also be used for private meetings, business functions, conventions, or other special events
- Special VIP area for business meetings, concert "meet and greets," etc.
- A center-hung scoreboard and multiple electronic scoreboards with advertising opportunities
- Roadside marquee viewable from the Robert M. Bell Parkway
Food and Beverage Services
- The USCA Convocation Center features two fully operational concession stands
- Several mobile concession stands accommodate food and beverage requirements for all events
- Catering for functions, such as a group meeting, can be arranged through our dining partner Aramark.
