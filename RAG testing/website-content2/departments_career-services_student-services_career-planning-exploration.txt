Begin. Build. Become.
Career planning is more than choosing the right major.
It's a lifelong process of setting goals and striving to reach those goals.
Things to consider when exploring which career is right for you:
- Interests
- Skills
- Values
- Geographic location
- Family
Trying to find work you will love? Get started by taking this quick career assessment!
This innovative, scientifically-supported career guidance tool
- uses a predictive fit measure to help you prioritize the best education and career pathways where you will find purpose and meaning in the world of work.
- connects you to real-time opportunities for internships and employment that align with your career fit.
What Can I Do With This Major?
Whether you’re exploring majors or searching for information about your chosen field, What Can I Do With This Major? will help you connect majors to careers.
Learn about typical career areas and types of employers that hire people with each major and strategies to make you a more marketable candidate.
What Can I Do With This Major? is produced by the University of Tennessee Center for Career Development
