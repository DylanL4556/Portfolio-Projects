Welcome
Under the direction of the Budget Director, the Budget Office seeks to:
- Assist all business officers in executing the board-approved budget.
- Assist principle investigators in adhering to grant-imposed budget and fiscal guidelines and complying with the policies, procedures, and documentation in conjunction with the generally accepted accounting principles of the state of South Carolina.
- Ensure that the university maintains a sound financial status and operates within the budget limitations established by the General Assembly and Board of Trustees.
- Provide essential support services that maximize the use of limited resources to meet the university’s mission.
