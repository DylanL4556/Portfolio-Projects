Private Shows
We are the perfect place for your next event! Private shows are arranged for educational groups such as scouts, preschools, and colleges and for fun events such as birthday parties and company celebrations. We even have Exotic Reptile Encounters to add to your experience. To schedule a private showing for your group, email or call 803-641-3313.

Private Show for USCA Faculty, Staff, or Students - $125
This extends the USCA discount to individuals working, or attending school, at USC Aiken who want to have a private party here. As a special bonus, this price includes the use of the lunchroom.
Group Rates
USC Aiken Sponsored Groups - $125
This rate is for showings sponsored by official USCA entities.
USCA Student Groups - Door cost: $50 minimum
Any student club or organization acknowledged by the student activities office is eligible for this rate. The door cost is $2.00 per USC-Aiken student (with ID). The rates for guests of USCA students fall under the normal price schedule for public planetarium shows.
USC Aiken Classes - no charge
Faculty wishing to bring their classes to the planetarium as part of a course may do so at no charge. Scheduling arrangements must be made with the planetarium personnel.
Other Educational Entities - $145
Groups from other universities or other educational associations.
Reservations
Reservations are based on staff availability. We need 2-3 weeks' notice, but we prefer four weeks. A $50.00 deposit is required to hold your reservation. The balance is due upon your arrival. Please make checks payable to USCA. An event may be canceled more than 24 hours in advance for a deposit refund minus a $5 handling fee. If no notice is given and no one shows up for an event, we will keep the $50.00 deposit.
Lunchroom
We prefer to present the show before refreshments if you use the lunchroom. You are welcome to come and decorate before your program. There is a full fridge/freezer available for your use. You can bring cake and ice cream, but other foods should be ordered through the Campus Dining Services (641-3293). You are responsible for removing all items from the lunchroom before leaving. Trash cans will be available.
