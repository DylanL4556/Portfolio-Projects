Ways to help your student.
There are many milestones in your student’s life, including career choice. Here are a few ways you can support your student along this path.
- Understand the current job market
- Offer support during the various career development phases
- Affirm the value of networking and gaining experience
- Help your student identify personal interests, skills, and talents
- Encourage exploration of all career options and opportunities
- Connect your student with people, places, and professions
- Find the right “parental involvement” balance
Here are a few services we offer to help your student make important, career-related decisions. Please encourage your students to visit us.
- Career Counseling
- Finding an Internship
- Résumé Review
- Preparing a Cover Letter
- Networking Events
- Mock Interviews
- Job Search
- Graduate/Professional School Planning
Students are able to make counseling appointments by visiting our office.
