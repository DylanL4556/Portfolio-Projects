Teaching Fellows | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/academics/soe/opportunities/teaching-fellows/
The USCA Teaching Fellows program is designed to cultivate future teacher-leaders through a comprehensive blend of scholarship, hands-on experience, and professional development. Fellows engage in a coordinated, collaborative program that includes leadership training, service learning, and action research. Early and ongoing field placements in diverse local schools help build essential classroom skills, cultural competence, and a reflective teaching practice.
The Teaching Fellows program at USCA empowers scholars to become confident, committed educators—and teacher-leaders—ready to make a lasting impact in South Carolina’s schools and communities.
