Freshman Orientation | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/student-affairs/orientation/freshman-orientation/
The freshman orientation program is designed for 2023 high school graduates or students who have graduated in the past few years.
The orientation program for incoming freshmen is designed to assist you with your transition into your first year of college. The orientation program will consist of an introduction to your chosen academic program, computer support, informative sessions about campus procedures and policies, how to get involved in campus activities, opportunities to meet faculty, staff, and other new students, curriculum, academic support services, degree requirements, and registering for Fall 2023 classes.
