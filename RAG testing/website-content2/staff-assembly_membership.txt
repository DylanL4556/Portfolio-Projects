Membership in the USCA Staff Assembly is available to all full-time, permanent, non-teaching support staff of USCA. The CEA requires no annual monetary fee-- just time and involvement.
Accomplishments
The Staff Assembly is working for YOU. Some of our accomplishments include:
- Appointing a SA representative as a voting member of Institutional Planning Cabinet (campus committee that recommends budget allocations).
- Increasing the number of Staff Employee of the Year Awards from 1 to 4.
- Establishing awards to recognize classified employees for community service and university service (currently not awarded due to non-allocation of funds).
- Worked to increase the monetary awards given to the four Staff Employees of the Year (from $500 to $1000 per award).
- Managing the distribution of SA Enrichment Funds for professional development.
- Established connection with State Employees Association to monitor and influence state policy.
