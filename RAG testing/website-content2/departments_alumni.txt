Greetings Alumni and Thank You!
We also want to invite those of you who “want to be involved” but have not had the opportunity to do so or aren’t exactly sure how you might participate to contact us so we can begin exploring some possibilities.
Please take time to visit the other pages on our website and see many of our current initiatives and opportunities for involvement:
- Leadership Boards/Councils/Teams
- Annual Events
- Scholarships
- Alumni Recognition Opportunities
We hope to see you and your family at some of the great events and happenings your alma mater is hosting this year. Be sure to update your alumni record so you don’t miss out.
Please do not hesitate to share your thoughts or ideas by emailing us at alumni@usca.edu. We look forward to hearing from you soon.
