More Info:
Dr. Zidan is currently the Chief Technology Officer at FuelX Innovation Inc. Previously, he served as an Advisory Scientist at the Savannah River National Laboratory in Aiken, SC. His research focuses on hydrogen storage, including the development and characterization of novel materials such as alanates, doped carbon nanotubes, amorphous metal hydrides, and conventional metal hydrides. Additionally, he is engaged in the development of hydrogen separation membranes. His interests extend to non-linear diffusion, chaotic behavior in non-linear systems, computational physics, molecular dynamics, Monte Carlo methods, percolation theory, and computer simulation of fractal-like systems.
