Councils
Councils serve USCA Alumni Association members by offering activities and events for alumni and friends. Each Council assesses the needs of the alumni in an area or affinity group and makes recommendations for activities and events. These activities may include social gatherings, community service opportunities, networking events, and fundraising.
The mission of all Alumni Councils is to support USCA and its alumni while fostering the university spirit in every way possible.

Get Involved in Other Ways
There are several ways to become involved with the Alumni Association. You can help organize one of our functions, serve on a planning committee, be a part of a fundraising team for USCA’s Annual Scholarships, or make a financial donation.
For more details on becoming involved, email the Office of Alumni Relations at alumni@usca.edu or 803-641-3480.
Contact Us if you:
- are interested in starting a chapter/council or serving on a leadership team in your area
- want to network/connect with other USCA Alumni
- would like to become more involved with your alma mater
