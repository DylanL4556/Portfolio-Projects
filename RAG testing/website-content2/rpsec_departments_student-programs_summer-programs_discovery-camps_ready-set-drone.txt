Drones are now being used in a wide range of career fields. From entertainment to environmental science, drone piloting is becoming a sought-after skill. In this camp, students would learn how important and fun drone piloting can be!
This STEM-themed experience harnesses the magic of drones to make learning fun. Students will be engaged in situations that encourage leadership, collaboration, and teamwork while learning about physics and engineering principles related to drone use.
Coding isn’t just trendy, it’s increasingly the foundation of technology in every industry. Drones are one of the best ways for kids to apply complex math and science concepts to real-world applications.
Students who attend should demonstrate skills in math and science, with an additional interest in STEM and computers
