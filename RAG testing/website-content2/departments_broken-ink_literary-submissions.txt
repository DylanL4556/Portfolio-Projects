About Literary Submissions
Broken Ink welcomes a variety of literary submissions including, but not limited to, poetry, prose, short stories, creative nonfiction, lyrics, and short play scripts. Due to space limitations in the magazine, the maximum word count is 2,500.
Complete the form below to submit your literary work for consideration. Note that a separate form must be completed for each submission.
