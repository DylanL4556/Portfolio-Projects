$ Earn While You Learn $
The program offers diverse opportunities to enhance your skills and prepare for a successful career in cybersecurity:
- Scholarships: Financial assistance is available for members of the Cyber Pacers Competition Team.
- Internships: Gain hands-on experience through internships with our regional partners.
- Campus Jobs: Engage in real-world technology operations through on-campus roles, including:
- Helpdesk Support: Assist students and faculty by troubleshooting technical issues and providing IT solutions.
- On-Campus Security Operations Center (SOC): Play a vital role in monitoring and managing cybersecurity threats, gaining hands-on experience in real-time threat detection, response, and mitigation.
- Regional Security Operations Center (RSOC): Participate in advanced cybersecurity operations, contributing to proactive threat analysis and defense strategies.
- Technology Services and Development (TSD): Work on innovative technology operations, support IT system enhancements, and assist in maintaining critical infrastructure.
- Center for Cyber Initiatives, Awareness and Artificial Intelligence (CCIA-AI): Collaborate on projects promoting cybersecurity education and awareness initiatives.
- And many more opportunities: Explore a wide range of additional opportunities to develop technical expertise and leadership skills across various areas of campus technology and cybersecurity.
