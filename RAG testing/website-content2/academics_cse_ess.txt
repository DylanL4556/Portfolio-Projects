Unlock the Science of Movement and Well-Being
Explore the dynamic world of Exercise and Sports Science at USCA, where a rigorous scientific foundation meets practical application. Our interdisciplinary approach integrates biology, chemistry, physics, and more, offering a deep understanding of how physical activity affects the body and mind. Explore how exercise can enhance physical fitness, prevent chronic diseases, treat injuries, and optimize athletic performance.
Hands-On Learning for Real-World Impact
Our curriculum goes beyond theory, emphasizing practical skills in assessing health risks, physical fitness, and crafting exercise prescriptions tailored to diverse needs—from wellness enthusiasts to elite athletes and individuals managing chronic conditions. Choose from specialized concentrations in fitness management, basic sciences, or allied health, each offering unique experiential learning opportunities through internships, research, and field experiences.
