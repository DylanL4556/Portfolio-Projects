Facilities and Centers
Empowering Students and Faculty to Innovate, Excel, and Shape Careers Through Hands-On Collaboration
USCA is proud to house diverse state-of-the-art facilities that serve as hubs of innovation and learning for faculty mentors and students across all academic disciplines. These Research Facilities and Centers provide invaluable hands-on experiences, empowering students to enhance their education and develop skills essential for their future careers. Students gain practical knowledge that bridges the gap between academics and industry by engaging in cutting-edge research, creative problem-solving, and collaborative projects.
Students majoring in STEM fields enjoy unique opportunities to work alongside industry professionals, collaborate with potential partners, and connect with future employers in specialized facilities dedicated to advancing science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. These experiences are pivotal in preparing graduates for real-world challenges and establishing long-term professional networks.
We encourage you to visit each facility and speak with the dedicated faculty members overseeing them to learn more about their resources, research opportunities, and ongoing projects.
