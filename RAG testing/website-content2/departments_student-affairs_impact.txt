The IMPACT program at USCA encourages students, faculty, staff, and the community to collaborate on meaningful community service projects and promotes responsible citizenship through involvement in such projects.
There are many ways to get involved!
IMPACT projects are community service projects offered throughout the semester at different Aiken area agencies. Past projects have benefited the Mental Health Association, the SPCA, Carriage Hill Nursing Home, and Aiken Parks and Recreation, to name a few.
Why Volunteer?
Volunteering has many positive benefits and outcomes:
- Learn more about yourself
- Expand your horizons
- Experience a different lifestyle
- Develop leadership and communication skills
- Promote a good cause
- Meet new people
- Explore a career through practical experience
- Make a difference in the community and the lives of others
