LIFE students are fully engaged in campus life, making the most of every opportunity. They show their school spirit by attending PUB events and getting involved in a variety of clubs, including the Council for Exceptional Children, LIFElong Friends, the Horticulture Club, and the Anime Club. Many stay active through intramural sports, building teamwork and friendships along the way. Some also joined fraternities and sororities such as Delta Sigma Phi and Phi Mu.
