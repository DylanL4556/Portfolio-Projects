Housing Cancelation | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/housing/current-residents/housing-cancelation/
Cancelation Before the start of the Academic Year:
- If written notice of cancellation is received by University Housing on or before June 15, 2024, the contract will be canceled, and the $125 housing deposit will be refunded as long as there is no further indebtedness to the university. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
- If written notice of cancellation is received by University Housing after June 15, 2024, the contract will be canceled, and the $125 housing deposit will be forfeited. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
- If written notice of cancellation is received by University Housing after July 31, 2024, the contract will be canceled, the $125 housing deposit will be forfeited, and the student will be charged a $250 “no-show” or cancellation fee unless the student fails to enroll. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
Early termination by a resident after occupancy:
-
- A student who withdraws from classes during the fall or spring semester must leave University Housing within 48 hours of the withdrawal. The student housing charges will be prorated by the schedule in the University Housing Community Guide and the $125 security deposit will be forfeited. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
- A student must fill out a Housing Contract Release Form to change to commuter status once a room is occupied.
- If the request is granted, the student’s housing charges will be prorated by the schedule contained within the University Housing Community Guide, the $125 security deposit will be forfeited, and a $1,000 cancellation fee will be assessed to the student’s account. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
Cancellation at the End of the Fall Semester:
-
- The contract may be terminated at the end of the fall semester only for the following reasons: withdrawal from USCA, graduation, marriage (no more than four weeks before the wedding date), and financial hardship caused by a change in financial status (documentary evidence will be required).
- A student qualifying for cancellation under section 10a will forfeit the $125 security deposit. If that student enrolls at USCA in the spring, they will incur a $1,000 cancellation fee. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
Cancellation Mid-Year (New Spring) Applicants:
-
- If written notice of cancellation is received by University Housing on or before December 15, 2024, the contract will be canceled and the $125 housing deposit will be refunded as long as there is no further indebtedness to the University. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
- If written notice of cancellation is received by University Housing after December 15, 2024, the contract will be canceled, and the $125 housing deposit will be forfeited. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
- If written notice of cancellation is received by University Housing after January 2, 2025, the contract will be canceled, the $125 housing deposit will be forfeited, and the student will be charged a $250 “no-show” or cancellation fee unless the student fails to enroll. The $25 application fee is non-refundable.
Housing contracts are for the full school year. Approval must be granted to move out after the fall semester begins. Your contract begins when you pick up your keys and sign your occupancy card.
Please review the Housing Contract Release Form carefully and note that you must be approved before moving out of University Housing.
Housing Deposit Refunds
Students who are not returning to University Housing should make sure their address is correct on their Self Service Aiken account. Only students who have successfully fulfilled their housing contract receive a deposit refund. Deposit refund checks (when applicable) will be mailed to you at the address listed on your Self-Service Aiken account, and they will take 6-8 weeks to process. Incorrect addresses will delay your receiving your refund.
