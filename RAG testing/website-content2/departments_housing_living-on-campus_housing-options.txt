Pacer Crossings Residence Hall
Pacer Crossings houses our incoming freshmen. Each suite contains four furnished bedrooms, four full bathrooms, and a furnished living room. Every bedroom also comes furnished with a mini fridge and a microwave. The laundry room is located on the second floor, and the Learning Center with computers, individual study rooms, and a printer is located on the third floor.

Pacer Commons Residence Hall
Pacer Commons offers apartment-style housing with double rooms and single rooms. However, single rooms are primarily for upperclassmen. Each apartment contains two bedrooms; two full bathrooms, a furnished living room, and a fully equipped kitchen. The laundry room is located on the first floor, and the computer lab is located on the third floor. There is a volleyball court behind Pacer Commons for easy access.

Pacer Downs Apartment Complex
Pacer Downs offers apartment housing with double rooms and double as single rooms for upperclassmen and graduate students. Double as a single rooms are available on a limited basis and are reserved for upperclassmen and graduate students. Each apartment contains two bedrooms, two full bathrooms, a furnished living area, and a fully equipped kitchen. There is a laundry room on-site, a volleyball court, a basketball hoop, and a furnished Community Center which houses the computer lab and printer.
