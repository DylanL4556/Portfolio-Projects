ChemDraw
ChemDraw can be obtained for free with a valid USCA email address. Click on the button below to visit the ChemDraw site. Then, create an account using your university email address. Once you are logged into FlexNet, you should see a link to ChemDraw Prime. Download the software by clicking on the link in the "File Name" column. Using the checkboxes to the left will launch a download manager that you don't want to install.
