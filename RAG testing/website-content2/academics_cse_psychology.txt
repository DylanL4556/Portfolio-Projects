Welcome to the Department of Psychology
Delve into the complex and fascinating world of psychology with the undergraduate and graduate programs at USCA. Our undergraduate curriculum offers a comprehensive exploration of the diverse subfields of psychology, combining rigorous academic study with hands-on research and practical applications. In a learning environment designed for individual attention, students not only absorb psychological theories but also actively participate in laboratory work and applied experiences, bringing these ideas into real-world contexts.

Elevate Your Expertise with Our Master’s Program
Our Master of Science in Applied Clinical Psychology goes beyond traditional boundaries to cultivate highly competent, ethical professionals who are ready to make a significant impact. This program equips you with the advanced knowledge and critical skills necessary to become a scientist-practitioner who integrates evidence-based and multicultural practices effectively. As a student here, you will develop not only professional capabilities but also the self-awareness and ethical grounding to influence positive change both within and beyond clinical settings.

Be a Leader in Your Community
At USCA, we encourage our psychology students to extend their influence outside the classroom and therapy settings, empowering them to become proactive leaders in their communities. Whether through engaging in groundbreaking research, participating in community service, or leading initiatives that advocate for mental health awareness, our students are prepared to make meaningful contributions to society.
