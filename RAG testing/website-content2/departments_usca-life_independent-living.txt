Independent Living | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/usca-life/independent-living/
Students live in Pacer Commons, a four-bedroom apartment-style dorm on campus. Three USCA LIFE students and one LIFE Resident Assistant (LRA) share an apartment. Every student has their own bedroom. Students share two bathrooms, a living room, and a kitchen. The LRA helps the students with independent living during the evening and on the weekends.
