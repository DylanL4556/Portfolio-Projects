We will go Full STEAM Ahead this summer to inspire young adventurers while focusing on science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics (STEAM) as access points to provide enrichment and enjoyment. Campers are given opportunities to engage in experimental learning, guiding student inquiry, and creative and critical thinking. The 2025 theme will focus on ADVENTURE.
The camp is made possible with support from the Generac Foundation.

Food
The Ruth Patrick Science Education Center will NOT provide snacks and Lunch. Campers must bring their snacks and lunch. Science Center educators supervise snack and lunch breaks.
Camp Check-In
All campers must check in and out with a parent or guardian daily. Any pick-up or drop-off changes should be turned in at that time.
Pick-Up
Parents or legal guardians must pick up their children at the end of each program. Children will not be allowed to meet parents outside of the pick-up area. To ensure the safety of campers, we require advance written notice when anyone other than a parent or legal guardian will pick up campers after the programs.
