The 2025 Scramble for Scholarships Annual Golf Classic
The 2025 Scramble for Scholarships Annual Golf Classic will be held on Monday, November 3, 2025, with a 9:00 a.m. shotgun start at the Woodside Country Club.
Details
The annual USCA Golf Classic is limited to the first 25 teams to sign up. All members of the community, not just alumni, are welcome to participate! The format is captain’s choice (4 person - best ball). Teams will be handicapped so that every team will have a fair chance to win.
Lunch will be provided by Chick-fil-A.
The deadline to register is October 27, 2025.
