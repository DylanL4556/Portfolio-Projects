Guest Policy | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/campus-recreation-wellness/facilities/guest-policy/
Guests are allowed to use the Wellness Center with a current member.
All guests are required to complete an informed consent and have a picture ID upon entrance to the facility.
The guest fee is $10 for a daily pass. The guest must come to the facility with the current member and sign in at the main desks of either facility. Please arrange to bring the correct amount for the guest pass.
Guests are allowed to use the Natatorium without a current membership. The guest fee is $10 for a daily pass. Guests can enter the Natatorium and pay for the guest pass with the lifeguards. All guests are required to complete an informed consent and have a picture ID upon entrance to the facility.
Intramural and Club Sports are limited to individuals that are enrolled students and current faculty and staff.
