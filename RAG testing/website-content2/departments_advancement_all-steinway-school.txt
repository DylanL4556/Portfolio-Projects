All Steinway School | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/advancement/all-steinway-school/
Nearly a decade ago, USCA embarked on a campaign to provide its students with world-class Steinway pianos. The first phase of the Steinway campaign was completed in the Fall of 2018, with the delivery of pianos in January 2019. As a result, USCA became the first public institution in South Carolina to be designated as an All-Steinway institution. This distinction demonstrates a commitment to excellence and an unparalleled educational experience by providing students with the incomparable touch and tone of Steinway pianos.
Beyond music studies, it is inspiring to see the pianos used by students throughout the USCA campus, who may not have an outlet for creativity otherwise. Arts organizations, such as the Aiken Symphony and Joye! in Aiken, benefit as well.
