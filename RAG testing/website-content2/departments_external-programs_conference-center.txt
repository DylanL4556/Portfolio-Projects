Conference Center | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/external-programs/conference-center/
The Conference Center at USCA offers services to accommodate your meeting and event needs, including renting an array of facilities with various options for room set-ups and seating capacities. These include classroom, theatre, or banquet-style rooms.
We are located in the Business and Education Building (Number 916 on campus) with parking in the “C" parking lot.
We aim to ensure that your meeting, seminar, or conference is a successful experience that meets your goals and exceeds your expectations.
Event Planning
Our office can assist you with planning your meeting space needs, arranging for catering, audio-visual services and equipment, and handling details such as room signs, etc. Local lodging information is available upon request.
Educational Resources
The USCA campus offers a wealth of knowledge through our excellent faculty resources. The Office of Continuing Education can assist you with selecting speakers or facilitators for your program. Continuing Education Credits (CEUs) are available for qualifying programs. You also have access to on-site information through the Gregg-Graniteville Library on campus.
Meeting Facilities
The USCA Conference Center features several rooms with various set-ups and seating capacities. Other facilities on campus may also suit your needs, and we will be glad to work with you to find the best space for your group.
