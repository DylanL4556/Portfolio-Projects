Purpose and Mission
The USCA Alumni Association is meant to support the mutual interests of the university and its alumni by fostering opportunities for each while serving, informing, and involving alumni in the educational process.
Membership Criteria
To become a member of the Alumni Association, all you have to do is make a minimum donation of $25 to any department, program, or scholarship of your choice while meeting one of the following criteria:
- Have obtained an associates, bachelors, or master's degree from USCA
- Be a former student who did not receive a degree
- Be a friend of the university who has expressed interest in supporting alumni activities
If you’re interested in becoming a member, make a gift to USCA. If you’d like to be active in our alumni community, learn more about Alumni Councils and Advisory Teams below.
Membership Benefits
Members of the Alumni Association have access to a broad set of benefits through a combination of PacerPerks and local offerings. For more information about membership benefits, visit the Alumni Benefits page.
Councils and Advisory Teams
Alumni Councils are designed to build relationships among alumni and create networking opportunities for participants. Each council determines its own needs and works with the Alumni Office and the Alumni Board to ensure the stated goals and activities are consistent with the purpose of the Alumni Association.
Interested individuals can serve on Alumni Advisory Teams in areas where councils do not exist. Members of these teams receive special communications from the alumni office and may be asked to advocate for the university and participate in discussions in person or online.
