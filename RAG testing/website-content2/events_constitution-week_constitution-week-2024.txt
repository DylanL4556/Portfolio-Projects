Constitution Week 2024
The Cold War and the Constitution
Each year, the Department of History, Political Science, and Philosophy celebrates Constitution Week by offering educational programming and voter registration opportunities to USCA and the Aiken community. This year, we examine the role of the Cold War and SRS in Aiken County’s history.
All events are free and open to the public
