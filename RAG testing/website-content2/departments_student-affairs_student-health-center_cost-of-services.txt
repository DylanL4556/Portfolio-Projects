https://www.usca.edu/departments/student-affairs/student-health-center/cost-of-services/
There is no cost for undergraduates to visit the Student Health Center. A nominal fee is assessed each semester and paid simultaneously as tuition. This fee covers the general care provided by the Student Health Center. Graduate students need only pay a $30 fee at the time of their first visit each semester. Nursing physicals are $50.
Fees for more specialized services are collected when these services are provided. Such services may include health physicals and over-the-counter medications.
