Make A Reservation | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/rpsec/departments/planetarium/make-a-reservation/
You are encouraged to make a reservation at least 24 hours in advance. You may make a reservation online, by e-mail, or by phone (803-641-3654). Please include your name, total number of guests, the date and time you wish to attend, and your phone number. You can make a reservation for up to 20 guests. If you want to bring a larger group, we encourage you to learn more about our private shows. All unclaimed reservations are released 10 minutes before the show.
Please note: Children under four years of age are not permitted in the planetarium for safety reasons and the consideration of other paying guests.
ADA Statement
Please indicate if you need any special services, assistance, or accommodations to participate in our programs by contacting us in advance at 803-641-3313 or by email. If you have visual or hearing impairment, please contact us at least three business days before your visit.
