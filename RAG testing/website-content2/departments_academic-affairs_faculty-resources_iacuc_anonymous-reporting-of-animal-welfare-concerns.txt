Concerns related to animal welfare at USC Aiken should be reported by answering the questions below. However, you may also express concerns in person or by phone or email to any member of the IACUC committee. All concerns will be address by the IACUC committee by assessing the facts and developing a plan to address any issue that is found to be not in compliance with approved protocols.
