Margaret J. Weston Medical Center
Address: 1211 University Lane, Aiken, SC, 29801
Phone: 803-648-4744
Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
Urgent & emergent care. Also, routine type care, i.e., health physicals & lab work.
Margaret J. Weston has a sliding scale pay system for lower-income patients (fee-based on income). You must take proof of your income or visit the employment office if you are not working for a statement that you were not working before your doctor’s appointment.
Appointments Needed? Yes, but walk-ins are accepted 8:00 - 10:00 a.m.
Payment Sources: Private insurance (Blue Cross/Blue Shield only), Medicaid, Medicare, Visa, Master Card, Check & Cash (No American Express or Discover).
