Why report community service hours?
IMPACT (USC Aiken Community Service Organization) adds up the total service hours from the previous month during the first week of each academic year.
After tallying up the hours, IMPACT will bestow two recognition awards for individual and organizational achievement.
How to enter your service hours
To enter service hours, each individual must create a Presence account. This is a fast and easy site that tracks every hour logged in.
REPORT COMMUNITY SERVICE HOURS
All of your service hours matter and will add up!
The Leadership Banquet takes place each March. We add up all the service hours from the present academic calendar year and present two awards (individual and organizational) for the most service hours for the academic calendar year.
