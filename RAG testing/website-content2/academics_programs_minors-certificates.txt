Purpose & Mission
USCA’s minors and certificates are designed to help students specialize, explore new interests, and enhance their career readiness. These programs add value to any major by promoting interdisciplinary learning and offering flexible, high-impact academic experiences.
Rooted in faculty mentorship and real-world relevance, our mission is to support a personalized education that prepares every student for graduate study, career growth, and lifelong learning.
