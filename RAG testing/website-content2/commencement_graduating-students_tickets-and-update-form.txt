Ceremony Tickets
For December graduation candidates, no tickets are required for family and friends; however, ALL graduates must have a ticket to enter the Convocation Center. Tickets can be picked up at the "Grad Finale" or the Alumni House.
For May and August, ceremony tickets are required for guests. Graduates must pick them up in advance at Grad Finale on April 16 or the Alumni House between April 17- May 7. The total number of graduating seniors and available seating determine the number of tickets allotted to each candidate.
