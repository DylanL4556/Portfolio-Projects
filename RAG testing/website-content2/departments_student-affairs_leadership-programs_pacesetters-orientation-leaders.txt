What is a Pacesetter?
The Pacesetters are orientation leaders committed to helping new students navigate their college experience at USC Aiken. They answer questions, distribute information, organize operational logistics, and facilitate activities. To be successful orientation leaders, Pacesetters participate in an exciting and educational leadership development program.
Pacesetters serve in a variety of roles, working with both Freshman and Transfer/Nontraditional Programs.
These orientation leaders are some of USCA's best students who exemplify leadership and a genuine passion for helping others.
