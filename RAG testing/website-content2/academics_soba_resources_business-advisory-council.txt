Business Advisory Council
The mission of the Business Advisory Council is to guide, promote, advise, and support the School of Business Administration in its efforts relative to the USCA in its efforts to serve its students, state, region, and nation. The Council advises and directs the school’s students, faculty, and administrators.
The Business Advisory Council officially meets twice per year. The agenda for each meeting is developed by the School's dean in consultation with the Chair and the council's membership. Other meetings are scheduled as needed to address the School's and University's needs.
