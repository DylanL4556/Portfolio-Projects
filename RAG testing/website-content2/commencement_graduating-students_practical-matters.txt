Practice
A practice and information session is held at the Convocation Center the morning of graduation, immediately after the Senior Brunch.
Ensure your name is correctly pronounced during the ceremony
On the day of graduation, students will have an opportunity to practice the pronunciation of their names with the commencement speakers. The speakers will phonetically spell out the names to ensure correct pronunciation. This is highly recommended if you think there is a small chance of mispronunciation. Once the ceremony time is set, more details about the time and location will be provided.
If your name has changed recently...
Name changes must be turned in 4 weeks before commencement to appear on your diploma. Please contact the Registrar's Office at graduation@usca.edu for more information.
What to wear to the ceremony
Students receiving bachelor’s or master's degrees wear navy-blue gowns and caps. The color of your tassel is determined by your degree. These items should have been purchased at Grad Finale.
Women are strongly encouraged to wear flat or very low-heeled shoes. Some women wear dresses, while others wear dress slacks. Men wear dress slacks, a dress shirt and tie, and dress shoes.
