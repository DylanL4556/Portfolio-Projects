Confidential counseling services are available for full—and part-time students. Call the Counseling Center at 803-641-3609 or stop by suite 107 in the Student and Educational Support Building during office hours Monday through Thursday 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m., and Friday 8:00 a.m. -12:30 p.m. to schedule an appointment with a counselor.
If you are having an emergency, please call 9-1-1. If you are on campus, please call University Police at extension 6111 from a campus phone or 803-648-4011 from your cellphone.
For 24/7 Mental Health Support
For an emergency or a crisis after hours, please get in touch with the following:
- Aurora Pavilion Behavioral Health Services RESPOND (803-641-5900 or 800-273-7911)
- The SC Department of Mental Health Mobile Crisis Access Phone Line (833-364-2274)
- 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline (dial or text 988)
- SAMHSA National Helpline (800-662-4357)
- The Crisis Text Line (Text HOME to 741741)
If you need additional supportive information, referrals, or information after hours, contact the Aiken County Help Line by dialing 211”
Other helpful steps you can take:
The Counseling Center offers free counseling services to full-time and part-time students and can address various concerns. Browse our topics and issues, and if you don’t see what you’re looking for, contact us and tell us what’s on your mind.
