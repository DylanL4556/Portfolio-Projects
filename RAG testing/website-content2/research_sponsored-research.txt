Purpose
The Sponsored Research Office at USCA is dedicated to supporting and advancing research activities through securing external funding and managing awarded grants. Our mission is to facilitate the growth of scholarly research by providing essential resources, guidance, and administrative support. We aim to streamline the process of obtaining research funding, ensuring compliance with regulations, and promoting the ethical conduct of research involving human subjects. By fostering an environment of collaboration and excellence, the Sponsored Research Office empowers researchers to achieve their scholarly goals and contribute meaningfully to their fields of study.
USCeRA
All proposals must be submitted through USCERA for approval and submission. This platform ensures proper account setup to manage awarded funds.
Proposals must include:
- Budget
- Budget Justification
- Scope of Work
The Director of Sponsored Research can review draft documents before submission. For a complete review by the USC Sponsored Research staff, submit through USCERA at least three working days before the proposal deadline.
