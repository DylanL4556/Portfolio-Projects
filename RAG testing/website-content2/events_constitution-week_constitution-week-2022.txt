Constitution Week 2022
The US Constitution a Global Perspective
Monday, September 12th - Dr. Mitchell Krumm
When Is Breaking a Constitution Right? Secession in Sudan, Canada, and the UK
When: 11:00 AM
Location: H&SS 103
Dr. Mitchell Krumm, Assistant Professor of Political Science and an expert in political theory, will discuss the ethics and realities of secession in the context of Sudan, Canada, and the United Kingdom.
Tuesday, September 13th - Dr. Chancellor Heimmermann
Constitution Week 2022 Keynote Address
When: 4:30 PM
Location: SAC Mezzanine
Dr. Daniel Heimmermann, Chancellor of USC Aiken, Professor of History, and an expert in French history, will present the keynote address.
Wednesday, September 14th - Dr. Samuel Pierce
Republics in Comparative Perspectives: The United States, Germany, and Spain
When: 3:00 PM
Location: Penland 106
Dr. Samuel Pierce, Associate Professor of History and an expert in Spanish history, will consider three Republics: The United States, Germany, and Spain in their comparative historical contexts.
Thursday, September 15th - Dr. Roger Deal
Global Constitutional Movements
When: 12:30 PM
Location: Penland 106
Dr. Roger Deal, Professor of History and an expert in Middle Eastern history, will explore Global Constitutional Movements in the 19th and 20th centuries.
