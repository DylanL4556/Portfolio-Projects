Program Highlights
Our program is a holistic and broad Communication program, and our classes are taught by award-winning professors with Ph.Ds who know how to design courses effectively and are always responsive to student needs.
- 100% of our student go through intense Career and Self-Development training.
- 100% of our students complete a Capstone Experience, which is considered a high-impact teaching practice and ensures our students are ready for the workforce and continued intellectual development and lifelong learning after graduation.
- 100% of our students complete a final portfolio or ePortfolio, which is a high-impact teaching practice.
- 100% of our students take courses focused on diversity and global learning, also a high-impact practice.
- Our students participate in undergraduate research and present at academic conferences.
- Communication degrees are some of the popular university majors in the United States because effective communication is essential to your personal and professional success.
