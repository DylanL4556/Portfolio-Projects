Pacer Commons | USCA
https://www.usca.edu/departments/housing/living-on-campus/housing-options/pacer-commons/
Pacer Commons residence hall houses 316 returning and transfer students. Rooms are equipped with two bedrooms, two bathrooms, a full kitchen with a refrigerator and stove, and a furnished living room with a kitchen table.

Features in Pacer Commons
- Share a two bedroom apartment with two bathrooms, or a four bedroom apartment with two bathrooms, furnished living/dining area, kitchen with full size stove and refrigerator
- Be within walking distance of classes, campus dining, activities, and many support services
- Live in a safe environment with 24-hour Public Safety coverage
- All utilities provided (except telephone)
- Academic Resource Center and Tutoring
- Onsite mail and package delivery
- Participate in educational and social programs planned by the Resident Mentors (RMs) to help you adjust to college life
- Pacer Net (Connect your computer in the apartment with wireless high-speed internet access or Ethernet Ports)
- On-site laundry facilities
- Tons of free parking spaces (But a parking permit is required - register at Public Safety)
- Enhanced security features including a "three door" system: Building Front Door (card access), Apartment Door (key access), Bedroom Door (key access)
- Ample common areas for recreation and study space as well as a game room
- Computer Lab with printers on the 3rd floor
- Snack and drink machines
