The USCA Eventing Team is a member of the USEA Intercollegiate Eventing Association. We compete in at least two collegiate challenges each semester, host and/or attend clinics at least once a year, and because Aiken is in the heart of “horse country,” there is ample opportunity for our members to compete and train individually in the area! We strive to bring students together to learn and train in the sport.
