On Campus Opportunities
Practice your language skills and meet peers in our monthly on-campus Spanish conversational tables.
Study and learn Spanish with our experienced language tutors at the Gregg-Graniteville Library.
Join Omicron Beta, the USCA chapter of the Alpha Mu Gamma National Collegiate Foreign Language Honor Society!

Enter the Modern World
Knowing a language other than English opens a variety of job opportunities. Some major fields where languages are an asset include education, healthcare, social services, travel and tourism, or media and communication.
Our faculty members and our programs excel in providing students with a global education. We encourage everyone to pursue the following opportunities abroad:
- North American Language and Culture Assistant Program in Spain
- Meddeas Language Assistant Program in Spain
- M·ximo Nivel Spanish Immersion Programs in Latin America
- Teaching Assistant Program (TAPIF) in France
- TEFL Certification Course and Language Assistant Program in France
