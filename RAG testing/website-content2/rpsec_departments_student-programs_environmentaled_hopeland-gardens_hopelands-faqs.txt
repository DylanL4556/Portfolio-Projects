Hopelands Gardens is located at 135 Dupree Place, Aiken, SC 29801. Please turn in at the Hopelands Gardens sign (not the Rye Patch). Parking will be on your left.
There is no charge for students or adults. Hopelands completely funds this program!
If you have confirmed registration for a program and find you must cancel, we MUST have written notification of your cancellation at least 4 weeks prior to the reserved date, or a cancellation fee will be assessed. This does not apply to cancellations due to weather or other natural events.
A maximum of 50 students per day (students rotate through 3 programs).
In the event of a delay or inclement weather, please call or text Kimberly Fickling at 803-300-4488.
- If it is only a light sprinkle, we may continue as planned and you should dress accordingly.
- If the weather is too cold or rainy and you need to cancel, please call or text Kimberly ASAP.
- If a trip must be canceled, we will try to reschedule for a later date if possible.
- The teacher will make the final call about canceling due to the weather. (No cancellation fee will be assessed.)
If your group plans to stay for lunch, participants should bring bag lunches and drinks. Lunch and restroom facilities are available at Hopelands. There are no concessions or vending machines.
Participants should bring a refillable water bottle and their lunch/drink in warm weather.

Hopelands Gardens (yes, both words end in an “s.”) and Rye Patch are wonderful historical and natural environments located within the City of Aiken. The pristine gardens, wetlands, and reflection pools are accessed by a series of lazy curving paths.
For more information about Hopelands Gardens and the Rye Patch, visit the City of Aiken's Parks website.
Field Trip Checklist
Before Your Field Trip:
- Confirm transportation arrangements and directions. Check with transportation about your latest departure time.
- Make lunch arrangements.
- Notify Kimberly Fickling if student numbers have significantly changed.
- Remind students to dress appropriately for the weather and to wear long pants and close-toed shoes.
- In warm weather, remind students to bring a refillable water bottle in addition to their lunch drink.
Before You Leave School:
- Pre-assign chaperones to groups.
- Count all of your students and all of your adults.
When You Get Back to School:
- Discuss the field trip with your students
- Visit the Student Programs section for post-visit activities for some of the programs.
- Return the teacher evaluation/feedback form if you did not leave it at Hopelands Gardens.
