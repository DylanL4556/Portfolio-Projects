Late stay in Housing for winter break
University Housing is no longer accepting late stay requests for the winter break. The deadline for late stay submissions was Tuesday December 9, 2025, at 9:00 am. If you submitted a request, please check your USCA email on Wednesday, December 10, 2025, for more information about your late stay request.
Please note, anyone requesting to sign-up for late stay after the sign-up deadline will be charges a $50 fee.
If you have questions, please contact Hope Smith-Dunbar at hopes@usca.edu or University Housing at housing@usca.edu .
