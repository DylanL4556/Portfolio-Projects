Congratulations on being offered the opportunity to apply for the USCA Local Resident Housing Scholarship. It is offered only to those USCA new students who meet the following requirements:
- Reside within 35 miles of the university and are not required to live on campus
- Will be enrolled as a student at USCA in the fall 2025 semester
- Earned a 3.0 or higher GPA in high school and/or your last institution
- Must have completed community service
Thank you for your interest in the Local Resident Housing Scholarship. The scholarship application has closed as of June 30, 2025 and no additional applications are being accepted at this time. All applicants will receive an email updating them on the status of their application and scholarship award. Please check the email address that you provided on the application.
If you have any questions, contact us at (803) 641-3790 or email housing@usca.edu.

About On-Campus Living
Once you are a resident on campus, you will be able to participate in all of the programs that help residents become academically successful and engaged with the university community. Living on campus helps to connect students to the campus in meaningful ways that will help them get involved in their studies, meet with tutors, advisors, and faculty, and get involved in activities.
