This dual coding and cyber security camp inspires middle-grade students through innovative, hands-on projects and challenges designed to propel them to Tech Readiness.
The Cyber portion of the camp is designed as part of the National Youth Cyber Education Program created by the Air Force Association (AFA) to inspire K-12 students toward careers in cybersecurity or other science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) disciplines critical to our nationís future. Campers will also learn basic coding skills by utilizing VEX Robotics. They will complete several engineering and coding challenges using the robotics equipment. Basic knowledge of computer hardware (knowing how to use a mouse, browse the internet, identify common computer icons, etc.) is helpful but not necessary.
Students who attend should demonstrate skills in math and science, with an additional interest in STEM and computers.
The camp is made possible with support from SRNS.
