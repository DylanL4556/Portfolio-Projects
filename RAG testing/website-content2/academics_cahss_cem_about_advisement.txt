Advising Philosophy
We adhere to what is often referred to as a holistic model of academic advising. A simple description of holistic advising is simply that an advisor creates an equitable relationship with his or her students and helps mentor the whole person in a tailored way.
We also adhere to NACADA’s core values: Respect, inclusivity, commitment, professionalism, empowerment, integrity, and caring.
