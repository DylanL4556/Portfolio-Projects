How to Nominate a Human and Civil Rights Champion
- All nominations for awards must be submitted on the official nomination form. The form may be duplicated.
- Nominations may be mailed or made online:
- Only one name may be submitted on a nomination form.
- Any person may submit a nomination for each of the three categories. (USCA Student, Aiken Tech Student, Aiken Community)
- Each nomination form can be accompanied by appropriate supportive materials such as:
- Resume
- Newspaper clippings
- Newsletter
- Programs
- Certificates
- Citations
- Photographs
- Class assignments
- Any person is eligible to receive the award regardless of race, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, or faith.
- Nominations must be received by 5:00 p.m., December 1, 2025. Winners will be invited to the January 25, 2026, MLK Event.
