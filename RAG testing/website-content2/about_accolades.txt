University of South Carolina Aiken is proud to be recognized by several prestigious organizations for our commitment to excellence in education. We have been honored by Colleges of Distinction for our engaged students, great teaching, vibrant community, and successful outcomes. Additionally, Military Times has acknowledged our dedication to supporting military members and veterans on campus, and US News & World Report has ranked us for our outstanding undergraduate programs and commitment to academic excellence. USCA has also been recognized as a Military Friendly gold-ranked school, and a Top 10 gold-ranked school for military spouses.
These accolades reflect our ongoing commitment to providing a top-tier educational experience.
