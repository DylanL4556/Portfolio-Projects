Natatorium Water Aerobic Reservations
Natatorium reservations are made online through IMLeagues. All pool users will be required to create an account on IMLeagues.
To create an IMLeagues account:
- Visit the IMLeagues website or download their mobile app.
- Click "Create Account" and enter your information. Use your university email if applicable (@usca.edu). Submit information.
- You will be sent an activation email. Click the link in the email to log in and activate your IMLeagues account.
- You should be automatically joined to your school. If not, you can search for schools by clicking the “Schools” link.
- If you do not have an @usca.edu email, request to join the USCA Network.
To sign up for a pool lane:
- Log in to your IMLeagues account.
- Click the “Facility Reservations” button at the top left of your user home page.
- Once the calendar screen is up, click on the date and time you want to reserve.
- Then, click the “Sign Up” button in the middle of the screen.
- Then, accept the waiver and click the “Sign Up” button to complete your reservation.

Registration to participate is required (or highly encouraged so you know you have a spot in the class). Drop-ins will be allowed if there are fewer than ten people signed up.
Reservations will be made using the IMLeagues app or the IMLeagues website www.imleagues.com or call the natatorium during operating hours at 803-641-3431. See the instructions above for registration procedures. When asked for the facility, type out “University of South Carolina Aiken.” If you do not have a usca.edu email account, you will be prompted to request access through USCA, which we will grant you.
Once registered with IMLeagues, please select Water Aerobics when making a reservation. Members can make a reservation up to five days in advance.
All Natatorium protocols must be followed upon entry into the facility.
Once you have an account, follow these instructions:
- Go to Facility Reservations.
- Select the day/date you want to make a reservation.
- Click Join Session.
- Read through the waiver and click the box to acknowledge you have read the waiver.
- Click 'Sign Up.'
You are all signed up for your class!
