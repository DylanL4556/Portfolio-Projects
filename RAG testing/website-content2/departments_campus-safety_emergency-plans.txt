The USCA Emergency Action Plan was created to guide the university community in saving lives, protecting property, and ensuring continued operations through preparedness, education, and individual readiness. This plan is a guide to assist in critical incidents. Because critical incidents are varied and unpredictable, adaptation must be exercised along with this guide to adjust for the unexpected. If you are approached by media during an emergency, please direct them to University Marketing and Communication at news@usca.edu or 803-641-2825.
