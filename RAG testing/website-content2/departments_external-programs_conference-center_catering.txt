Notification required for catered events
USC Aiken and Aramark Catering Services require advanced notice to accommodate a catered event:
20 business days - Notification must be provided that catering will be needed for your event with menu selections submitted.
14 business days - Notification with the final guest count for catering must be provided.
Note: Cancellations made 48 hours or less before the event start time will result in a full catering invoice.
Menu options
Menu options are available on usca.catertrax.com. However, clients may not order catering directly from this website as all orders must go through the USCA Conference Center.
Disposables
Catering costs include disposable plates, bowls, cups, napkins, and cutlery as required.
Plates, silverware, and glasses
Aramark can provide these items at an additional cost. Clients must request these additional items 14 business days before the event start date.
Linens
Linens can be provided for your event for an additional cost. Please notify us if these are needed.
Select a meal format
Aramark provides various meal formats, including buffet, plated and boxed lunches.
Special cakes
Aramark can provide a cake for your event if this is specifically on your catering order within the appropriate notification timeframe. You may also receive authorization to order your own cake only if it is provided by a professional bakery. Cakes and other food may not be brought in from non-professional sources.
