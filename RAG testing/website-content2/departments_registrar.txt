Our Mission
The Office of the Registrar's purpose is to assist, direct, and provide accurate information related to academic records and registration and other areas related to student course enrollment.
Our goal is quality customer service to all students, faculty and administrative staff, and the general public.
This office has supportive staff members who strive for excellence, while promoting a friendly and wholesome atmosphere. We are here to serve your administrative and registration needs.
If you are having trouble finding something on our website, take advantage of the search bar in the top right-hand corner or give us a call. We will be more than happy to assist you.
