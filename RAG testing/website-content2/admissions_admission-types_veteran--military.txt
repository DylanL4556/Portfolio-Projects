Why enroll at USCA if you are a veteran or active military member?
- Dedicated Veteran Services: Benefit from dedicated resources such as the Office of Veteran and Military Student Success (VMSS), which offers support from admissions to graduation and ensures a smooth transition to academic life.
- Generous Credit Transfer: Maximize your military training and experience with generous credit transfer policies, allowing you to apply relevant learning towards your degree and potentially shorten your path to graduation.
- Flexible Learning Options: With flexible scheduling and online programs, active service members can continue their education without disrupting their service commitments.
- Strong Community: Join a community where military service is respected and supported, providing a strong network of fellow veterans, active service members, and civilians who understand and appreciate your service.
- Military Times Recognition: As a university honored by Military Times as one of the best colleges for veterans, USC Aiken demonstrates a proven track record of providing quality education tailored to the unique needs of veterans and active service members.
