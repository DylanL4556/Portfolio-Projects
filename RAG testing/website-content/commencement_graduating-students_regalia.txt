Ordering Your Regalia (Cap and Gown)
Cap and gown orders are currently being taken through the Jostens USCA website. Your grad regalia will be shipped to your home.
Bachelor's Packages: $138.55
Master's Package: $199.50
Place your order by October 26, to ensure arrival before the ceremony. The website will not be available after this date as the items will not arrive in time if not ordered by then.
If you have any questions regarding cap and gown orders, please email Ken Duthie at ken.duthie@usca.edu.

The Chancellor's Medal
Degree candidates who qualify for graduation with honors from the university will wear the Chancellor’s Medal. This medal is a gift from the university to those students who have achieved the highest academic excellence. If you have been notified that you will likely graduate with honors, please pick up your medal from the Office of the Registrar on the morning of Commencement. For more information, please get in touch with the registrar.
Pacer Pursuit Medallion
The Division of Student Affairs will distribute the Pacer Pursuit Medallion. For more information, contact Ahmed Samaha, Vice Chancellor of Student Engagement and Belonging.
