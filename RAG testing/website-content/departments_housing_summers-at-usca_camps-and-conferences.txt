USCA University Housing Minor Policy
We are committed to protecting the welfare of minors who participate in programs that are held/present on campus. This policy has been established to ensure appropriate supervision and protection of minors on campus during programs, regardless of location.
Any outside organization with participants under 18 will have to follow these policies while utilizing summer housing at USCA. Failure to follow all procedures within the Minor Policy will result in possible termination of reservation.
After submitting your organization reservation form, a University Housing staff member will send the appropriate documents that will need to be completed by all minor participants before arriving on campus (i.e., Waiver of Liability and Media Release, Health and Emergency Information, Consent Form for Medications and Medical Devices).
Eligibility for Summer Housing
Housing for the summer semester applies to all groups with a single organization making payment for accommodations. Groups may include interns, camps, conferences, or competitions billed on one invoice. The application must be completed to reserve the dates for your organization. Once completed, the contact person listed will receive a notification of availability from University Housing.
Information on Residential Building
Unless stated otherwise, group housing will be offered in Pacer Crossings during the summer 2026 semester. Pacer Crossings is a furnished suite-style residential building with four bedrooms, four bathrooms, a closet, and a living room. Suites are provided with a mini fridge and microwave in each bedroom. Please visit our main page to take a virtual tour of the building.
Features in Pacer Crossings
- 24-hour University Police coverage
- Wireless internet capabilities and streaming services
- On-site laundry facility (payment required to use)
- Ample parking
- Outside pavilion area
- Two large meeting areas on the 1stand 4thfloor
- Two hallway kitchens on each floor
- All utilities included (except telephone)
Assignments
Groups requesting to stay will make their own room assignments. Indicate the number of desired rooms on the reservation form. Changes to room spaces needed should be communicated to University Housing and Residence Life ASAP and are not guaranteed due to limited space.
Check-in and Check-out Information
Check-in will begin at 8:00 a.m., and check-out will be up until 5:00 p.m. in the Pacer Crossings office on the date submitted on the application. Any organization wishing to check in before the time indicated must have written approval from the Director of Housing and Residence Life seven days prior.
Housing for camps, conferences, and competitions will be available starting mid-May 2025 to end of July 2026. Dates are subject to change.
