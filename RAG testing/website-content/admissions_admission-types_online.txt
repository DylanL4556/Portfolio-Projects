Since 2013, the University of South Carolina Aiken has leveraged cutting-edge technology and dedicated faculty to deliver diverse online degree programs. Our low student-to-faculty ratio ensures personalized interaction and support, allowing students to study flexibly from anywhere at any time.
Why Choose USCA for Online Studies?
- Flexible Learning: Study on your schedule from anywhere in the world, balancing your education with personal and professional commitments.
- Engaged Faculty: Benefit from personalized attention and support from dedicated faculty, ensuring a rewarding and interactive online learning experience.
- Affordable Excellence: Access high-quality education at competitive tuition rates, making advanced learning more accessible.
- Diverse Programs: Choose from a wide range of online degree programs designed to meet various academic interests and career aspirations.
- Career Advancement: Leverage career-focused programs and resources to enhance your skills, expand your professional network, advance in your current field, or pivot to a new career.
Requirements
All online applicants must submit the following items:
- Online application with a $45 non-refundable application fee
- Official transcripts from all universities attended, if applicable (Form)
- Test scores (List of Accepted Scores)
- Proof of citizenship (Form)
For Palmetto College, our online degree completion program, applicants must:
- Have 45-60 credit hours from a regionally accredited university and completion of specific courses.
- Have a cumulative GPA minimum of 2.0 for business programs and a minimum of 2.75 for special education programs.
