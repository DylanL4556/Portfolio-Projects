Program Highlights
At USCA, our Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting program is accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB). It also helped our school to be named one of the country’s Best Colleges for Business by Colleges of Distinction. This means that earning a Bachelor’s in Business Administration in Accounting at USCA will be highly regarded and valued in the field.
As a business accounting major at USCA, you’ll:
- Discover fun and supportive student groups. As a Business Accounting major at USCA, you’ll find myriad student groups and events that suit your interests, including the Pacer Accounting Club, the Student Advisory Board, the annual Business Leadership Symposium event lectures, and others.
- Prepare for career-related certifications. Our BS in Accounting program is aligned with state requirements for the Certified Public Accountant (CPA) exam, so you know it will help you develop the skills you need for licensure.

What can you do with a Business Accounting degree?
Accountants play a unique and essential role in business, and with a Business Accounting degree from USCA, you can, too. With a BS in Accounting, you can positively contribute to decision-making in any business arena through measuring, reporting, interpreting, and evaluating financial information.
