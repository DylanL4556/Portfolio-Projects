PacerPerks
PacerPerks is a benefits program for all USCA Alumni Association members. It provides discounts at participating restaurants, retailers, travel locations, and more across the country. With deals ranging from Subway to Edible Arrangements to Disney World, membership will pay for itself in no time!
How to sign up
To sign up for PacerPerks, you must first become a member of the USCA Alumni Association. Once you sign up, you should receive a welcome email with a user-specific code to create an account within 48 hours. After creating an account, it’s as simple as entering a zip code to find discounts in the area!
