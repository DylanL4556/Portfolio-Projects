Shaping World Citizens
Through a student-centered and communicative approach to second language acquisition, our programs are fundamentally targeted towards the workforce and integrate a set of skills that is interdisciplinary and applicable to our modern world. Relevant projects, hands-on activities, real-life scenarios, on-campus events, and an experiential learning education equip our students with creative and critical thinking abilities for a successful career after graduation.
Our Programs:
BA in Spanish Language, Literatures, and Cultures

BA in Spanish-English Translation & Interpretation

Minor in Spanish-English Translation & Interpretation
