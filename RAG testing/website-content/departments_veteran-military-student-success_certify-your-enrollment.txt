Please complete your enrollment certification for the upcoming semester(s) to notify the VMSS officials of your use of educational benefits. If you are attending classes through USCA, online or on campus, you can submit the online certification below before the start of each semester. This is necessary so that the Office of Veteran and Military Success can certify your courses to the VA Education and Training Department.
More information about education benefits offered by VA is available on the official U.S. government Website at http://www.benefits.va.gov/gibill.
If you need to submit a prior term request, please email us at vmss@usca.edu.
