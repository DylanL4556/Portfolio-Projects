Families are welcome at USCA all year, but one fall day is when we pull out all the stops to make your visit extra special. We invite all Pacer families to register for and attend Family Day, traditionally held in late September or early October. From check-in to the Chancellor’s Brunch to activities like Aiken’s famous historic trolley tour, you and your student will enjoy a special time reconnecting at USC Aiken!
Career Services
Whether your student is just starting classes at USCA or they are finishing this year, they should introduce themselves and stay connected with the Career Services department. Events and activities throughout the year will help students find internships, permanent employment, or a job while they are in college.
