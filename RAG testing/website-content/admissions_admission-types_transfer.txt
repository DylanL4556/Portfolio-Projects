What kind of transfer student am I?
Transfer
If you have attended another college-level institution and attempted one or more courses, regardless of credit earned, you are a transfer student. If you have attempted less than 12 semester hours of college-level work, you must meet both transfer and freshman entrance requirements.
Readmit
You are a readmitting student if you have missed a major semester (fall or spring) or more at USCA or any other USC campus. If you have attempted work at any other non-USC institution, you must submit transcripts from those institutions.
Change of Campus
If you are currently enrolled at another USC campus, you are a change of campus student. Students with less than 12 semester hours of non-developmental coursework must also meet freshman admissions requirements.
Transient Admissions
You are a transient student if you are attending another college or university and would like to enroll at USCA for one regular semester or two summer terms before returning to your college or university.
Non-Degree Seeking
If you wish to take a course(s) for personal enrichment or professional enhancement, you are a non-degree-seeking student. Entrance exams, transcripts, and recommendations are generally not required.
