Parenting your college student
Parenting (or supporting) college students is not for the faint of heart! It takes patience, understanding, and compassion while also standing your ground. Acknowledging these challenges, we have gathered the following books and articles that may help you to guide your Pacer.
If you have book or article recommendations to share, please send that information to Families@usca.edu
Recommendations:
Don’t Tell Me What to Do, Just Send Money by Helen E. Johnson & Christine Schelhas-Miller
The Naked Roommate: For Parents Only by Harlan Cohen
50 Tips for Parents of College Students by Alan Farber and Linda O’Brien
"Parenting Secrets of a College Professor" by Kathleen Volk Miller
