Constitution Month 2025
South Carolina and the Constitution
Each year, the Department of History, Political Science, and Philosophy celebrates the constitution by offering educational programming to USCA and the Aiken community. This year, we examine the South Carolina Constitution and the role the state has played in shaping US constitutional history.
All events are free and open to the public, registration is not required.
