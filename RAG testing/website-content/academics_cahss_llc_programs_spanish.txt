Unlock the advantages of multilingualism with a Spanish degree from USCA.
Bilingual employees are in high demand, with Spanish being among the most useful languages to be fluent in. Our major in Spanish at USCA focuses on immersive experiences so you can converse comfortably and confidently with Spanish speakers, as well as the cultural contexts to appreciate this rich language fully.
Students pursuing a Bachelor’s Degree in Spanish can choose between two concentrations:
- Spanish Language, Literatures, and Cultures
- Spanish-English Translation & Interpretation
Both tracks include the opportunity for an immersive study abroad program, as well as interesting coursework taught by native Spanish and English faculty.
Program Highlights
When you major in Spanish at USCA, you’ll benefit from:
- A two-week study abroad opportunity. Master your Spanish with an immersive trip to Spain during the “Maymester” term. Spanish majors spend two weeks studying at Universitas Castellae in Valladolid, Spain. Not only will you take intensive Spanish courses, but you will also stay with a Spanish-speaking host family. This experience includes cultural experiences in the evening and a guided excursion to Segovia over the weekend.
- A diverse faculty. Our faculty’s research interests encompass the entire Spanish-speaking world, from the Iberian Peninsula to Latin America, the Caribbean, and even the Philippines. You will be taught by both native Spanish and native English speakers, giving you exposure to different perspectives for approaching the language and cultures of the Spanish-speaking world.
- Small class sizes. The Spanish section of the Department of Languages, Literatures, and Cultures at USCA is small and intimate. Upper-level Spanish courses can have as few as six to eight students. You will have the chance to form connections with familiar faces as you complete your Spanish degree.
- Opportunities for internships. If you major in Spanish Translation & Interpretation, you can explore the many jobs available for Spanish speakers by completing an internship. These internships are directed by our Translation & Interpretation specialist and provide hands-on experience using Spanish in the professional world.

Course Highlights
The major in Spanish at USCA is designed to give you mastery of the language, as well as the cultural knowledge to communicate effectively and fluently. Spanish majors take core classes to learn advanced vocabulary and grammar, become comfortable in conversation, and use the language in the real world.
Choose the concentration that caters to your interests
Are you interested in diving into the nuances of the Spanish language, the history of the Spanish-speaking world, or the diverse literature it has produced? If so, the concentration in Spanish Language, Literatures, and Cultures may be the right track for you.
Through this track, you will enrich your understanding of Spanish language, literature, and culture with courses in:
- Latin American, Peninsular/Iberian, and Caribbean literature
- The civilizations and cultures of Latin America and the Iberian Peninsula
- The histories of the Spanish-speaking world
For those wanting to pursue a career in Spanish-English Translation & Interpretation, this track prepares you for a variety of interesting jobs. You will learn techniques to help you bridge the gap between English and Spanish speakers through courses that delve into the theory and practice behind interpreting and translating.
This track includes courses that explore a variety of professions, including:
- Education
- Medical
- Business & Finance
- Public Service
