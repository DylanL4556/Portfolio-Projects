Your Launchpad for Professional Success
We’re committed to guiding you from your initial inquiry to your final steps toward launching a successful career.
Career Services
Our Career Services department provides a comprehensive suite of resources to prepare you for the workplace:
- Personalized Counseling: One-on-one mentorship to help you clarify goals, refine your strengths, and navigate your career path.
- Internship Placements: Partnerships with leading employers to secure real-world experience that complement your studies.
- Job Placement Assistance: Workshops on resume writing, mock interviews, and access to career fairs and networking events.
- USCA Handshake: Our online job-posting board featuring internships, part-time roles, and full-time positions for students and alumni.
- USCA Graduates for Hire: A dedicated portal connecting USC Aiken alumni with employers seeking proven talent.
Admissions & Enrollment
Begin your journey with an easy, supportive admissions process:
- Application Guidance: Clear step-by-step instructions and key deadlines for all
- Financial Aid & Scholarships: Personalized assistance to identify grants, loans, and scholarship opportunities.
- Orientation & Support: Programs designed to welcome you into our community and set you up for success from day one.
- Admissions Advising: Direct access to advisors who can answer questions and streamline your enrollment.
