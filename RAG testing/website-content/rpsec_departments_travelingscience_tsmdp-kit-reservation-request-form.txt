Traveling Science and Mathematics Demonstrations Program - Kit Reservation
Requests should be made at least 1 FULL WEEK prior to the date you need a kit.
Please be aware that the kit you request may not be available.
You will be notified via e-mail (please provide correct email address below) when your reservation request has been confirmed or denied.
You may also call 803-641-3683 to make reservations.
Kit reservations are limited to 2 week (14 day) periods*. You can always extend, if it’s available, by calling 803-641-3683 or emailing travelingscience@usca.edu near the end of a reservation period.
