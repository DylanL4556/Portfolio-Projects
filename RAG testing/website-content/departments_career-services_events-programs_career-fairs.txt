Career & Graduate Program Fair
Wednesday, March 4, 2026
10:00 am - 1:00 pm
Student Activities Center Gym
Attending a career fair is invaluable for job seekers looking to embark upon their careers. It provides a unique platform to network with a diverse array of potential employers, learn about different industries, and explore various job opportunities all in one place. Career fairs offer direct access to recruiters and hiring managers, allowing attendees to make a lasting impression beyond the confines of a traditional job application. Additionally, they present the chance to gain insights into the latest trends and demands in the job market, enhancing one's knowledge and readiness for future career endeavors.
