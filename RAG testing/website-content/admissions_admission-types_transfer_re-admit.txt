You are a re-admitting student if you have missed a major semester (fall or spring) or more at USCA or any other USC institution. If you have attempted to work at any other non-USC institution, you must submit transcripts from those institutions.
To apply for admission, Re-admit applicants must:
- Complete an application for admission
- Submit the $10 application fee
- Submit official transcripts from all colleges attended since the last semester of work within the USC system through the time of application; to be supplemented by final transcripts to reflect complete academic work prior to beginning classes at USCA. Official transcripts must be sent directly to USCA from the issuing institutions. Documents handled by the applicant or anyone else are considered unofficial.
Applications and all supporting documents for the fall semester must be submitted by July 15 and for the spring semester by December 1.
Note: USCA allows for the automatic readmission of US military service members and reservists who are temporarily unable to attend class or have to suspend their studies due to service requirements. For more information, contact the Director of Admissions at admit@usca.edu or 803-641-3366.
