In this two-part camp students will interact with VEX Go Robotics for half of the day and then use our computer lab to design video games in Roblox Studio and Make Code Arcade. Student will learn to collaborate with teammates and design innovative solutions to global problems, all while using the VEX Robotics GO System. Learn to program your robots to complete several rover missions throughout this week of camp. Campers will combine coding, construction, and robotics as they work through the engineering design process to develop unique and inventive solutions to rebuild houses, plant and weigh crops, lift the water tower, and more.
Students who attend should demonstrate skills in math and science, with an additional interest in STEM and computers.
The camp is made possible with support from SRNS.
