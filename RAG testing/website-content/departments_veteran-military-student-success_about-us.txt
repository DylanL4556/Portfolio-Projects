The Veteran and Military Student Success (VMSS) center officially opened on April 7, 2013. USCA dedicates the center to the success of all past, present, and future military members and military family members enrolled as students.
The VMSS is your one-stop shop on campus for answering your questions about benefits, education, and career choices. The VMSS is also a place to meet and connect with, hang out with, or study with other students who share similar life experiences.
The VMSS is located in the Penland Building, Room 107. We are open from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Monday through Thursday, and 8:00 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. on Fridays.
