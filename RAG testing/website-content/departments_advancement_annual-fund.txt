Your gift to the USCA Annual Fund makes a difference in the lives of our students and our community. An investment in the future of USCA will undoubtedly benefit the communities of this region for years to come. The annual fund incorporates major fundraising initiatives, these include but are not limited to The Alumni Annual Fund, The Pacer Parent Fund, The Friends Fund (community members and businesses), The Family Fund (faculty, staff, and USCA retirees), and Giving Tuesday.
The USCA Annual Fund invites you to show your USCA pride by supporting your chosen academic department or program. Your commitment enables the university to raise academic standards and shows your belief in the future of USCA. Your commitment provides the University with the resources to make this campus even better which enhances the value of your college degree and makes a positive difference in students' lives.
How to Give
For more information on the ways to give and the ways you can designate your gift, please contact the University Advancement Office at 803-641-3408.
You can make your gift to USCA online through the USCA Online Giving site! Our website accepts all major credit cards, including American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa. You may also designate your gift to specific areas at USCA, and you can give in memory or honor of someone.
After entering your gift amount on the first page, be sure "Aiken" is listed under your gift designation on the second page. Thank you in advance for your support!
