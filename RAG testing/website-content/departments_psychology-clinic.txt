Clinic Services
The USCA Psychology Clinic offers a wide range of high-quality, evidence-based psychological services. Individual and family therapy are available for people of all ages dealing with various problems such as life transitions, anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship challenges, grief, and childhood behavioral issues. We also provide comprehensive assessments for ADHD, learning disorders, autism, cognitive functioning, and psychological diagnoses.
Who We Serve
The USCA Psychology Clinic is dedicated to serving everyone, including USCA students, faculty, and staff, and communities across South Carolina and Georgia. In addition, we provide training in the delivery of psychological services to advanced graduate students in the USCA Applied Clinical Psychology Master’s Program.
Contact Us Today
Our clinic is staffed by advanced graduate students in the USCA Applied Clinical Psychology Master’s Program as well as the licensed mental health clinicians who supervise them. Each member of our team is dedicated to providing compassionate and effective care to help you meet your goals.
