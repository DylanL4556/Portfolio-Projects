Benefits for ROTC
ROTC, Reserve Officer's Training Corps, is a program of military instruction that takes place on 270 college and university campuses nationwide. The origin of Military Instruction in Civilian colleges dates back to 1819. Army ROTC is one of the best leadership courses in the country and is part of your college curriculum. During classes and field training, you will learn first-hand what it takes to lead others, motivate groups and how to conduct missions as an Officer in the Army. Upon graduation from Army ROTC, you will earn the bar of a Second Lieutenant and become a leader for life.
