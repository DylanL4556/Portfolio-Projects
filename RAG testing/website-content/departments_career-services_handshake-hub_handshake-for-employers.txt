New to Handshake?
Handshake is very user-friendly. It’s easy to create a free employer user account and connect with USCA using the following instructions. Please get in touch with us directly if you have any questions.
- Visit their registration page, enter your corporate email address and desired password, and click “Sign Up.”
- Enter the following information, then click on Next: Employer Guidelines. None of this information will be made public to students unless you decide to make your profile public.
- First Name
- Last Name
- Phone Number: This is a free text field, format as desired
- Job Title: This is your job title and not the job that you are recruiting for
- Types of students you’re interested in recruiting on Handshake
- There is no way to add to this list, and your selections do not hinder your ability to recruit students; it's used for data purposes.
- Alma Mater & Graduation Year: If your alma mater is partnered with Handshake, this will create a contact entry for you at your alma mater, listed as an alumni contact.
*Note: Do not select which schools you’re interested in recruiting from here.
- A popup will load entitled Handshake’s Employer Guidelines and will include Handshake’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Click on either Yes or No after the question “Are you a third-party recruiter working on behalf of another company?” then click Next: Confirm Email.
- The next page contains instructions on verifying your account via your provided email address. The email should arrive within 10 minutes and contain a link to confirm your account. You must click this link before you can proceed with the registration process.
*Note: Please see Handshake's Confirmation Email Troubleshooting Guide if you haven’t received a confirmation email.
- Click the Confirm Email button to verify your account when the email arrives.
- You will be brought back to Handshake when you click Confirm Email in your confirmation email. You’ll need to connect with an existing company profile or create a new one if yours doesn’t exist in our system. The determination is based on the email you signed up with.
- If your company already exists in our system, the screen will display a list of company profiles associated with your email domain. If there is more than one, review the details for each profile. “Click Request” to connect with that company profile and then select Next: Connect to Schools.
- If you cannot find the correct company profile to connect with, click “Contact Us” under the “Not your company?” heading on the right. This will allow you to submit a ticket to our Support Team for further assistance.
- You can use the search bar to find your company if no company pre-populates. If your company does not exist, click the “Create New Company” button on the right. For more information on creating a new company profile, refer to Handshake’s How to Create a New Company Profile.
- Once you’ve requested to connect with or created a new company profile, you will be prompted to choose schools to connect to. You may search for our school name in the search bar at the top of the list.
- USCA’s name in Handshake appears as “University of South Carolina-Aiken.”
- Finally, click Next: Finish.
- The company profile connection process determines the next page.
- If you requested to connect with an existing company that has not configured automatic approval, your request will be sent to the company profile owner for review. For more information, please refer to Pending Approval to Join Company.
- If you request to connect with an existing company that automatically approves new teammates based on matching an email domain, you'll be taken to the Handshake homepage and prompted to set up Applicant Status Messaging.
- Suppose you have requested to connect with an existing company and create a new company profile. In that case, you’ll be taken to your Handshake homepage and prompted to set up Applicant Status Messaging.
