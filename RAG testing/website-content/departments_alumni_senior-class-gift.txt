Reins of Legacy
A Senior Class Giving Society
The Reins of Legacy is USCA’s senior class giving initiative that establishes a tradition of giving to the university.
By joining the Reins of Legacy, you show past, present, and future Pacers that USCA made a difference in your life and now you want to make a difference in the lives of others.
To become a member, you must contribute $25.00 or more and pledge to contribute the same amount or more over the next two years.
As a Reins of Legacy member you will receive:
- Membership in the USCA Alumni Association with full benefits.
- Reins of Legacy Philanthropy Cord
- Reins of Legacy membership certificate and gift.
An invitation to a private Reins of Legacy Induction Ceremony for you and your family. The ceremony will be held on November 12, 2025, at 6:00 p.m. in the SAC Mezz.
There are many ways that you can spend $25.00. Why not use it to make a difference in the lives of your fellow Pacers.
