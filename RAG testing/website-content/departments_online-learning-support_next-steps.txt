Congratulations again on your admission to USCA Palmetto College! There are several steps before you begin classes. You can begin working on the following items:
1. Set Up Your University Accounts and Email
All incoming students are required to activate their University Accounts and USC Aiken Email. These accounts provide access to essential university systems, including:
- Self Service Aiken – the central university hub for course advisement, registration, financial aid, tuition billing, final grades, and access to Blackboard, the university’s online learning platform where students view course materials, submit assignments, and communicate with instructors
- USC Aiken Email – the official channel for university announcements, academic updates, and important deadlines
Setup instructions are available in your Pacer Portal. If you do not see them listed in your checklist, please contact the Office of Admissions at 803-641-3366 for assistance.
2. Submit the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
Early submission of your FAFSA can be crucial. You should apply as soon as possible to receive consideration for financial aid. USCA's financial aid school code is 003449. Your award will appear on Self Service Aiken. For more information, visit the Office of Financial Aid website.
3. Participate in the Mandatory Orientation
USCA students are required to complete orientation before registering for courses. The orientation is a self-paced orientation course offered in Blackboard. Students who do not participate will not be advised or registered for classes. Details will be sent via email.
4. Citizenship
You need to verify your citizenship (if you have not submitted the FAFSA). You will not be able to register for courses until your citizenship verification is complete.
5. South Carolina Residency
Students who are lawfully present in South Carolina or in Richmond/Columbia Counties in Georgia and believe they may qualify for in-state tuition and state scholarship eligibility must complete the State of Residency Clarification Form. This form is available exclusively through your Pacer Portal.
To learn more about lawful residency requirements, please refer to the Citizenship & Residency Requirements guidelines.
6. Have AP/IB/CLEP/Other placement test scores sent to USCA (if applicable)
USCA's school code is 5840. For information on scores we accept, visit the Accepted Academic Credit Transfers page.
7. Send Transcripts
If you are currently enrolled in courses, please have your transcript sent to the USCA Office of Admissions once you complete your final semester.
8. Sign-up for Advisement and Registration
Advisement will be conducted via phone, email, videoconferencing, or face-to-face. Online Advisors are trained to work specifically with online students.
9. Prepare for the Semester
Information regarding your specific program can be found on the Palmetto College website or in your orientation course on Blackboard.
Palmetto College students do not have to meet the immunization requirement nor attend an on-campus orientation.
