Learn about the vast STEM career opportunities from real-world STEM experts. Discover different job roles in STEM. The area’s top experts will guide students through different career paths and how to get started. Participants will participate in hands-on workshops designed to help students learn in a safe and fun environment and do what STEM professionals do daily. Participants will also learn directly from our mentoring STEM professionals as they travel to various on-site locations. Throughout the week, the campers will be immersed in the STEM professional community.
*Letter of recommendation required for this camp. This letter can come from a teacher, mentor, or STEM professional.
With Support from Workforce Opportunities in Regional Careers (WORC)
