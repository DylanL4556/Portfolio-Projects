Throughout the year, the Office of Alumni Relations is asked any number of questions about services available to alumni at USCA, privileges they enjoy on campus, and University business. Answers to some of these questions are given below. The address for all USCA offices is 471 University Parkway, Aiken, SC 29801.
The Office of Alumni Relations is located in the Alumni House (the historic Pickens-Salley house) and is open from 8:00 a.m.-5:00 p.m (M-F). Our telephone number is 803-641-3408.
Any USCA graduate can become a member of the association by making a tax-deductible monetary contribution annually in any amount to the university through the Aiken Partnership. Checks or money orders may be mailed to USCA Alumni Relations Office, 471 University Parkway, Aiken, SC 29801.
You may also give your gift online. When completing the online form be sure to select USC AIKEN for the Campus, so your gift comes to your alma mater.
You can make gift designations on the memo line of your check or online or simply give to the area of greatest need. Within a short time, you will receive an alumni identification card, car decal, and a thank you letter of receipt for your tax records.
Alumni can easily update their information through this online form or by emailing the Office of Alumni Relations at alumni@usca.edu. Although name changes due to marriage, divorce or other reasons can be updated in the alumni database, official name changes on your USCA course record must be made and verified through the USC Registrar’s Office in Columbia. The telephone number there is 803-777-5555.
As an added benefit, current giving members of the USCA Alumni Association with an unexpired membership card receive access to the library and its resources. Members must go to the library and present their Alumni Association card to obtain a library card. There is no fee for borrowing books, and alumni are subject to the same library policies as students. For more information, call the Library at 803-641-3465.
