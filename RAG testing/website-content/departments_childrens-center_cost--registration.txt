Waiting List Policy
The center maintains a waitlist for all classrooms; you are not obligated to place your child’s name on the list. However, first priority is given to enrolled families expecting a sibling.
Second priority is given to children of full-time faculty or staff employed at USCA.
If you are interested in getting on our waitlist, please complete the waitlist form.
