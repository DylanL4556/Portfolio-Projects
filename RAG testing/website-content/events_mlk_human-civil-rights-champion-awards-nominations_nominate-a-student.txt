About the Human & Civil Rights Student Champion Awards
Two (2) awards are to be presented to a student from USCA and Aiken Tech – (1) from USCA and (1) from Aiken Tech.
To be eligible for the Human & Civil Rights Student Champion Award, one or more of the following criteria must be met:
- Helped protect and advance the human and civil rights of students on campus.
- Worked to build inclusive and diverse partnerships with students and faculty on campus
- Participated in a campus project or assignment that encourages the value of collaboration and protecting the rights of all in their campus community.
PLEASE NOTE: the form below is for nominating a USCA student for the Human & Civil Rights Student Champion Award. To nominate an Aiken Technical College student, please visit the ATC website and fill out their nomination form.
Nominations must be received by 5 p.m. December 1, 2025.
