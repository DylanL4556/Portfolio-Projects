Program Highlights
When you major in marketing at USCA, you will gain the overall skills employers value. Our marketing concentration coursework helps you become an invaluable asset to any organization.
At USCA, marketing degree students benefit from:
- An accessible and affordable program. When you major in marketing at USCA, know that you’re doing so at the most affordable four-year, accredited school in South Carolina. Our students also have access to additional scholarships and grants—making your studies even more affordable.
- Gaining essential industry skills. You will not only learn the skills to get ahead in business, but throughout your marketing degree program, you will have opportunities to prepare for industry credentials such as Microsoft and Project Management certifications, among others and SAS Tier 1 Academic Specialization certifications.
- Faculty expertise and mentorship. Your faculty advisor will assist you with career options and preparation. The School of Business Administration also has a director for the Center for Student Achievement to help marketing degree students prepare to enter the workforce after graduation.
- Experiential learning. As you pursue your bachelor’s degree in marketing, you’ll have opportunities to participate in faculty-led study abroad programs, semester-long study abroad trips, independent student research projects, internships, symposiums, and lecture series with executive speakers. The director of career readiness will help you through the internship process and assist you as you prepare to enter the workforce.
