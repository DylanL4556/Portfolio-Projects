Show your Pacer how much you care with a Pacer Care Package! These packages are a simple, thoughtful way to send encouragement and comfort right to campus.
We’re offering four themed packages, each with the option to include a personalized note from you:
🍿 Snack Attack – A mix of favorite treats like popcorn, chips, pretzels, candy, granola bars, and more!
🧸 Snuggle Bundle – Cozy comfort items including a stuffed animal, mug, journal, hot chocolate, fuzzy socks, and a sweet dessert.
💌 Peace Parcel – A calming collection with tea bags, a mug, colored pencils, a coloring book, and relaxing eye masks.
☀️ Daily Delight – Everyday essentials like toothpaste, a toothbrush, mouthwash, body soap, deodorant, hand sanitizer, chapstick, and gum.
Packages are $10 each, and the contents may vary slightly.
Please note: Students will receive an email with instructions on when to pick up their package from the Student Life Office. The estimated pickup date is December 1.
Pacer Care Packages are available for both commuter and housing students!
All orders are due by November 10.
