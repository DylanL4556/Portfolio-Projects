General
The Box Office is open Monday – Thursday from 8:30 a.m. to 6 p.m. and Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m.
The Etherredge Center will contact you by phone, letter, or both.
The lobby will open one hour prior to a performance. Patron seating will begin 30 minutes prior to the start of the performance. Patrons may arrive at either one of these times.
Patrons are allowed into the theatre during breaks for applause only. Some patrons may be asked to wait until intermission if their arrival time exceeds 30-45 minutes after the performance has begun. Also, late-comer seating may be at the discretion of the artist by contract.
