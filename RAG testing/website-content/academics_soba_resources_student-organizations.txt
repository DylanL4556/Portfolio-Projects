Beta Alpha Psi
Founded in 1919, Beta Alpha Psi is an honor organization for financial information students and professionals. More than 300 chapters are on college and university campuses, with more than 300,000 members initiated since Beta Alpha Psi's formation. All chapters are AACSB and/or EQUIS accredited.
Faculty Advisor: Lindsay Griswold
Beta Gamma Sigma
High-performing students will be invited to join this prestigious honor society each semester. The USCA Chapter of Beta Gamma Sigma was officially created on November 20, 2003. Beta Gamma Sigma, established in 1919, is the only scholastic honor society recognized by AACSB International.
Faculty Advisor: Julie Steen
Council for Business Students
The Council for Business Students empowers student engagement in the planning and implementation of programs and initiatives within the School of Business, on campus, and in the community.
Faculty Advisor: John Engel
Pacer Accounting Club
The Pacer Accounting Club is an organization for USCA students with concentrations in accounting and finance. Our objectives are to promote the study and practice of accounting and finance, provide members with opportunities for professional development, present valuable information to help students launch their careers, and give back to our community through service projects.
Faculty Advisor: Lindsay Griswold
