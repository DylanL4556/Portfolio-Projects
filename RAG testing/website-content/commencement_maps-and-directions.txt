Convocation Center Address
Address: 2049 Champion Way, Graniteville, SC 29829
Guest arrival and parking
Guests should enter the center through the front lobby. Guests must be seated by 5:45 p.m. The doors to the arena area will close at 5:50 p.m. for the processional, and the ceremony will begin at 6:00 p.m.
There is one area of the parking lot designated "by invitation only" for members of the Platform Party. Parking is available at the Convocation Center, and overflow parking will be on the main campus and you should use the pedestrian bridge to safely cross the Parkway. Please follow the direction of the USCA Police that evening.
