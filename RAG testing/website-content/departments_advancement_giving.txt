Scholarships
Scholarships make a tremendous difference in the lives of our students. The Advancement staff can work with you to give to an existing scholarship or begin a new scholarship.
Endowed scholarships create a permanent fund. Contributions are invested to ensure the scholarship retains its value in perpetuity, and the scholarships created by the endowment are funded through the income generated.
Annual scholarships reflect a donor’s commitment to support a scholarship for a specific number of years and choose the criteria in which recipients are chosen.
You may also contribute to an existing scholarship fund that matches your passion. Contact Mary Driscoll at MaryD@usca.edu or 803-641-3448.
You can make your gift to USCA online through the online giving site! We accept all major credit cards, including American Express, Discover, MasterCard, and Visa. You may also designate your gift to specific areas at USCA, and you can give in memory or honor of someone.
