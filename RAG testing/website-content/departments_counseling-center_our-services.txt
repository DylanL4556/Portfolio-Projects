The Counseling Center shares expertise in the areas of human behavior and development through consultation and outreach services. Professional counselors are available to assist students with a variety of academic and emotional issues of concern. Counselors are also available to consult with faculty and staff on student-related issues. Counselors can provide referral resources for faculty and staff who need counseling services.
Outreach efforts also include availability to make presentations on specific topics for classes, campus organizations, and other groups at the University upon request.
Services Offered at the Counseling Center:
- Individual counseling
- Brief therapy
- Crisis intervention
- Medical and psychiatric referral
- Study skills counseling
- Time management counseling
