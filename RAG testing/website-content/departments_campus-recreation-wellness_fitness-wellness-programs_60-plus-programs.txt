The USCA Wellness Center and Natatorium has a wonderful program dedicated to the senior population.
The mission of the Older Adults/Silver Sneakers Program is to enhance the quality of life of the participants by providing guided programs embracing healthy lifestyle choices.

Schedule
There are a variety of classes geared toward the older adult. These classes include Water Aerobics, Better Balance, Yoga for Seniors, Zumba Gold, and stretching classes. Please see the Group Fitness Class Schedule for a complete list of times for each class.
Class Descriptions
Natatorium Classes
- Classes in the Natatorium are included in the Natatorium and/or Wellness Center membership. Another option is to purchase a 10-class punch card for $30.
Stretch Gold
- A series of stretching exercises will be performed to improve joint flexibility.
Strength Gold
- A strength class geared toward the older adult.
Yoga Gold
- Geared toward the older adult, this class will focus on balance, strength, and flexibility. A chair can be used during the class.
Water Aerobics
- Take your workout to the water!
Zumba Gold
- Zumba focuses on the older adult and low-impact movements.
Chair Zumba
- All the fun of Zumba in a chair.
Chair Yoga
- Geared toward the older adult, this class will focus on balance, strength, and flexibility in a chair.
